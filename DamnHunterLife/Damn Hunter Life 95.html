[“Leaderrrrr!”]

[“Why did you say it was my skill! Why! I asked you to say it was Sa Ye-ho’s skill!”]

“You want me to lie to the entire nation?”

“Think about your situation. You should welcome people acknowledging you. If you want to retire and receive a pension.”

After the Gate Break erupted last November, the whole world began slowly adapting to life with Gates. And so, today marks the 9th month since Gate generation began.

The World Hunter Association finally succeeded in connecting the most special and secret otherworld where Korean hunters gather with the global comprehensive system.

And with that, finally all hunters on Earth were placed on the same system.

World rankings were registered.

Overnight, suddenly, all hunters including successors could see their rankings laid bare.

Of course, regular hunters also called Hunter School students didn’t have their rankings disclosed unless they revealed them personally…

The situation was different for successors whose stats were already public.

‘The world ranking format is insane.’

A Korean man in his 20s named Park Ho-gyeong, facing the massive data ranking site uploaded by the World Hunter Association, followed this behavioral pattern:

First, find Korean successor names in the world ranking top 30 posted on the first page.

====

2nd place… China/…

3rd place… USA/…

4th place Woo Sae-geon Korea/22 years old/Earth type

And feel ecstasy at Woo Sae-geon’s existence, who placed 4th and carved Korea after the USA and China.

‘This is so satisfying, ha.’

There’s analysis that Woo Sae-geon’s skills become sharper and stronger as he goes through Stages. Experts said the Woo Sae-geon from the Busan Gate a few months ago and the current Woo Sae-geon are completely different people.

Even now, that high growth trajectory shows no signs of breaking.

As much as this was true, it was also an element that made one lament the terrible flaw of Korea Hunter School’s ‘age limit.’ The moment such a person loses all awakening abilities and retires within this year is coming. It could be called a great tragedy that Korea faces.

Park Ho-gyeong scrolled down and discovered one more Korean name in the top 30, letting out a cheer.

====

15th place Ja Hyeong-woo Korea/21 years old/Water type

====

‘Hyeong-woo hyung, I always believed in you.’

And returning to the top 30 to look again…

There were no more Korean names. At this point, Park Ho-gyeong rapidly cooled down.

‘Let’s just search by Korean nationality.’

When searching Korean nationality, only the rankings of 10 people called successors appeared. Whether there was Korean Association coordination, they were listed in zodiac animal order rather than alphabetical or Korean alphabetical order.

As expected, all successors were S-rank.

Also, glancing at the numbers, besides Woo Sae-geon and Ja Hyeong-woo, the other successors also seemed to be positioned roughly within the 100-200 rank range.

‘Korea is unexpectedly a hunter powerhouse.’

Of course, Korea has the dependency that all other hunters use the successors’ power… But still, all successors being S-rank is nothing short of perfect.

Whether the order of animals and abilities really had a correlation, the first two figures were overwhelmingly strong and the rankings gradually decreased toward the back. Park Ho-gyeong checked the successors at the back with a bored expression.

“Huh?”
