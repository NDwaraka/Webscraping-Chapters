Damn Hunter Life Side Story Chapter 10

* * *

The Successor office at Hunter School where the fourth leader since Sa Ye-ho’s retirement was seated.

Since the stages disappeared, it was quite rare for serious voices to flow from the office until late at night.

“……You’re saying the ‘Tiger’ succession has been damaged.”

The 4th generation Successor leader, Jin Seol-woon, was lost in thought at his desk.

After Myo Cho-hui’s retirement, all wood attribute hunters were currently forced into a rest period.

Since it was revealed that Beom I-ryeong wasn’t actually a successor, the wood attribute that had almost become two people was reduced back to one. Even if they wanted to transfer affiliations to be active, there were no other wood attributes.

‘Since Beom I-ryeong was a false successor, the next succession would likely be the Tiger again.’

That’s what he had thought until now. That a Tiger successor would soon appear and the wood attribute’s rest period wouldn’t last very long.

But through the system’s hint, it was revealed that this belief was wrong.

Beom I-ryeong had dealt a blow to the successors even in death. The system said that because Beom I-ryeong had messed up things related to the Tiger successor, ‘Tiger’ succession might never happen again.

“……Do you remember how unstable our combat power was when there wasn’t a single metal attribute?”

Jin Seol-woon suddenly murmured quietly as if talking to himself.

“I’m not sure.”

The person sitting across from him hesitated and shook his head slightly. It was Hae Mi-jun, the next leader and colleague he had been working with.

At that time, Hae Mi-jun was excluded from the combat team due to sluggish growth, so he didn’t know the successors’ situation as well as Jin Seol-woon.

Jin Seol-woon, who reopened his sharp eyes, added.

“Think about the Busan Gate. Even considering that we were inexperienced, we were definitely lacking in overall ability then.”

Successors must always maintain balance, and their powers are all connected. This was a fact directly proven by Gyeon Gang-hyeon before stepping down from the leader position.

In other words, for overall power to have stability, and to exert greater power using each other as stepping stones, all five attributes must be activated.

“In that sense…… it’s not good now. The wood attribute has been empty for too long.”

After a successor retires, some time is needed for a successor with the same power to emerge. It’s for the circulation of power.

The other wood attribute, the ‘Cat’ successor. That side had proper circulation when Myo Cho-hui retired. In other words, the order was pushed far back.

If wood attribute awakening is delayed like this until a Rabbit successor appears again.

In the past, no one would have thought it strange even if one attribute was empty. However, the current successors were those who had already tasted their golden age under Gyeon Gang-hyeon’s generation.

Jin Seol-woon thought it might seem like they were in decline now. Indeed, from his perspective as leader, not only successors but regular hunters’ growth was subtly breaking and gate rotations were becoming frequent.

If a high-grade gate appeared at this rate.

‘I need to find a way to somehow restore the system so a Tiger successor can appear.’

Or a way to make five attributes exist without Tiger succession.

Jin Seol-woon, whose brow was slightly furrowed, soon rested his chin lightly. A low murmur leaked between his lips.

“What would Gang-hyeon-ssi have done?”

There had been Woo Sae-geon as leader before Gyeon Gang-hyeon, and Sa Ye-ho after, but Jin Seol-woon muttered as if he didn’t know this fact.

Actually, Hae Mi-jun sitting across from him was the same. They only thought of one person.

Soon Jin Seol-woon showed an effortless smile.

“Active generation matters should be handled by the active generation. I’ll think about related issues gradually.”

“Yes. I’ll also let you know if I think of any good methods.”

* * *

A few months ago. When Han Hyeong-woo and I had just started living together.

We were eating breakfast and sitting together watching the news when familiar words filled the screen.

“So this is actually happening.”

Hunter Olympics. Though it was already two years ago, the US had invited Korean successors to a different dimension for a friendly match as a test.

Gathering participating countries, gathering spatial ability awakeners to create stadiums. Thus, the Hunter Olympics, officially opening after two years, was scheduled to be held this summer.

“Korea is also a participating country.”

“Successors?”

“No, regular hunters.”

While casually talking with Han Hyeong-woo, the faces of Ma Cheon-ah and Hae Mi-jun briefly passed through my mind.

Some of them might happily go out.

But the current successors’ position was that they wouldn’t step onto an international stage. Therefore, they yielded the participation rights to regular hunters.

‘When even the current active members are avoiding it, what would retirees like me and Ja Hyeong-woo do?’

I just need to watch as a spectator.

So I had set aside the Olympics matter……

As time passed, on a day in early summer, I received this invitation.

「We would like to invite those five from that time to the opening ceremony. You don’t need to watch the subsequent competitions, so please attend the opening ceremony and grace us with your presence.」

The World Hunter Association invited the five successors from the past friendly match as VIPs.

“Why us?”

It seems like it would be bothersome if we go.

It’s not like international relations would become strange if we refuse this.

‘Didn’t they also invite us just for courtesy? So we can just refuse, right?’

However, I was overlooking something at this time.

The fact that there was one guy who would show boundless interest and curiosity.

[“Let’s go! Let’s go let’s go let’s go let’s go—.”]

GO Ye-ho machine-gunned my eardrums through the phone. I covered my ears with my fingers.

“Go ask Yoon Sae-geon to go!”

[“Sae-geon hyung said he won’t go. Won’t you go with me? Won’t you? Won’t you?”]

“Ah, go pester Yoon Sae-geon!”

[“Even for me, there are lines I can’t cross. Sae-geon hyung has his reverse scale too.”]

His lively voice suddenly became gloomy. I flinched for a moment.

‘Damn guy, using a sympathy operation.’

I don’t know what circumstances there are, but GO Ye-ho was always like that. He seems to come and go in Yoon Sae-geon’s life well, but then sometimes he’s cautious like that.

When I told him to go with Ma Cheon-ah, she said she couldn’t come because she had to go on an overseas gate expedition.

‘I thought she’d come, but has she matured?’

Eventually, the only person GO Ye-ho could go with was me……

Finally, I changed my mind.

“Hyeong-woo, want to go to the opening ceremony? I feel like going.”

“Huh? Yeah.”

Though I changed my mind in just 20 minutes, Han Hyeong-woo obediently nodded.

“But…… are you going because Ye-ho asked you to?”

Take back what I said about obedient.

“No? I want to try getting a suit fitted.”

When I spoke honestly, Han Hyeong-woo’s eyes became gentle again. Simple guy.

Since it was a bit dangerous for the two of us to go to a crowded department store together, we found a custom tailor shop with Manager Park Yoon-seok’s help.

Han Hyeong-woo followed because I was going. He looked happy until I tried on all kinds of suits, but when I said let’s measure your size, his energy suddenly dropped.

“I don’t like it because it’s uncomfortable.”

I whispered quietly in the ear of the guy throwing an unreasonable tantrum.

“If you get a suit fitted, we’ll be wearing couple outfits there?”

Then he immediately went to the side and pointed to the most ordinary black suit. Really simple.

But what he said to the staff right away was.

“Two of the same—”

I covered Han Hyeong-woo’s mouth and dragged him away. Han Hyeong-woo obediently let himself be dragged.

“Hey, you really want to wear the exact same thing? No way.”

I had tried to entice him for nothing and got it thrown back at me before long. I explained several times that if we appeared wearing the exact same thing, it would be like coming out to the World Hunter Association.

Han Hyeong-woo didn’t insist anymore. Instead, he became subtly sulky.

I ended up digging my own grave again.

“Pick whatever you want to dress me in for mine. Okay?”

Thus, the opening ceremony outfit Han Hyeong-woo carefully chose for me became a beige suit with a design that was just barely formal-like.

I wanted a darker color but couldn’t help it.

‘Still, as long as Han Hyeong-woo is happy……’

A few days later, several suits arrived at home. As soon as I opened the box, I told Han Hyeong-woo to put one on and come out.

Then I sat on the sofa and waited, but my mood was a bit…… strange. It reminded me of a groom-to-be or something.

“Do I look okay?”

Han Hyeong-woo came out after a while. He asked quietly while avoiding eye contact, seemingly embarrassed.

Though he was a bit too burly, his broad upper body and thick thighs made him look very tall and impressive. I inwardly let out an “oh……” in admiration and said.

“You look like a muscular bear in a suit.”

Han Hyeong-woo was visibly disappointed. Only then did I smile and pat his shoulder with my hand.

“No, your face is handsome but your shoulders and chest are too big.”

“Should I not wear it……?”

“Why wouldn’t you wear it? Wear it. I like it.”

I quickly appeased Han Hyeong-woo who was alternately looking at the mirror and his body.

“Then, Gang-hyeon, you come out wearing yours too.”

“No. I’ll wear it on the day.”

Actually, it was because I was already embarrassed thinking about how he’d sit on the sofa and stare at me dreamily if I came out wearing it. So let’s reduce the frequency to just once.

The opening ceremony, befitting an event with the Olympics name, had many spectacles and was magnificent.

I just went pretending since they casually invited us…… but honestly, I stared like a country bumpkin throughout the opening ceremony.

This would also be part of Cheon Gang-hyeon. Since he was always in the hospital, he probably never saw such things directly with his own eyes.

While I looked down at the opening ceremony venue from the VIP seats, Han Hyeong-woo watched me from the seat next to me.

For the opening ceremony, I had tied my shoulder-length hair in a half-up style, wore the bright-colored suit I bought with Han Hyeong-woo that fit perfectly, and put on brown shoes. I wore a metal watch on the inside of my wrist and lightly took care of my skin.

Maybe that’s why. Han Hyeong-woo’s gaze felt burdensome.
