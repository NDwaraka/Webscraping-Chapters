Damn Hunter Life Chapter 130

“But, Myodusa… is it waiting for our strategy meeting right now…?”

Hae Mi-jun asked an absurd question.

It seemed like that because the Myodusa had been staring at us intently until the moment we finished preparing for battle.

Instead of scoffing, I just slightly raised the corner of my mouth. Hae Mi-jun stared at my face as if entranced.

“It’s not waiting for us. Where would those things have such manners?”

No matter how cute the cat head looks or how dignified it seems, that’s absolutely impossible. They’re just things dying to kill living humans.

“I can see blue energy slowly rising. That thing just has disgustingly slow skill casting.”

Moreover, the giant serpent body visible behind its head also looked quite heavy-bottomed. Like it was trapped in some cave, the Myodusa didn’t budge from its spot.

Still, since we overcame the acid smoke with Ja Hyeong-woo’s curse skill, the situation isn’t entirely dark.

“Just be careful of the terrain and we can handle it. Let’s go!”

I shouted loudly once and then grinned.

For a moment, a strange atmosphere flowed. It felt like the mood suddenly turned awkward.

‘Ah shit, without realizing it. When Woo Sae-geon was right there behind me…’

But the silence was brief. Hae Mi-jun followed along with a bright smile.

Sa Ye-ho twitched his lips. Behind him, Jin Seol-woon rolled up his torn sleeve to his shoulder with a gently relaxed expression.

And Ja Hyeong-woo… when our eyes met, I was the one who stiffened a bit instead.

He was smiling. It was somewhat awkward, as if he was trying to copy my mouth corner. Like that smile I saw in the gate before, it made people flustered.

While gauging the magic stone’s location with my eyes to catch the Myodusa, I casually sent a chat to Woo Sae-geon.

==

Me: Leader, join the attack too

==

Woo Sae-geon moved smoothly without a word. Leaping nimbly like a beast to transfer to the next island and summoning his ox familiar to attack the Myodusa, he looked as terrifying as usual.

‘But he’s not giving this and that command like before. Is it because of mental fatigue?’

Even though the leader stayed quiet without saying a word, the strategy proceeded smoothly. Thanks to training and combat experience, both the successors’ individual capabilities and their ability to coordinate attacks by exchanging signals with teammates had greatly improved.

They were no longer puppets who could only move after Woo Sae-geon ordered every skill use and managed their offense and defense.

Myodusa wasn’t a threatening enemy except for spewing smoke. Especially since its movements were slow and sluggish, it wasn’t difficult to defeat. By targeting the head instead of the snake body surrounded by hard shell, we could easily bring it down.

“…The strategy success notification isn’t appearing.”

“That probably wasn’t the boss. No wonder it was easy.”

Instead of the quietly standing Woo Sae-geon, Sa Ye-ho collected the magic stone from the dead Myodusa.

Actually, I was more concerned with something else than the boss whose location I didn’t know. My heart kept feeling stuffy and I kept getting angry.

“Ah, I don’t know. Even if it’s out of context, I don’t care. I have to say it.”

I’ve been waiting for just this moment.

I gestured for Ja Hyeong-woo and Hae Mi-jun not to follow, then stepped back alone and stood by myself.

So that only I would appear in the system camera filming the strategy video, without anyone else being captured together.

Woo Sae-geon had blocked all reporters from us so that other successors younger than him wouldn’t make careless claims defending him regarding his controversy.

Even so, there’s one place Woo Sae-geon can’t block.

Right here, the stage battle scene.

I looked up diagonally. The direction of looking up at the sky. As far as I know, this angle faces the stage system cam directly.

Viewers watching the video later will make eye contact with me through the screen.

“The leader isn’t that kind of person. Saying the leader is that kind of person is insulting all of us successors who elevated that person to leader.”

I’m just as pissed at people who left comments agreeing with the articles criticizing Woo Sae-geon as I am at those fucking bastards from whatever drug organization.

How can they throw around such irresponsible and frivolous words? Especially Korean people, to Woo Sae-geon? How dare they, to Woo Sae-geon?

People criticizing Woo Sae-geon’s personality is quite satisfying. I’m probably included in that too.

“But I don’t know about other things… I can’t stand by and watch them belittle the devotion that person showed for Korea.”

Because of my statement just now, I might also receive criticism along with him until Woo Sae-geon’s innocence is properly revealed. I don’t care. Feel free to disparage me or brand me as an illegal defender.

I’m a delinquent who thinks it’s better to lose everything I have rather than live unable to say what I need to say.

Before I knew it, I had been clenching my teeth and speaking passionately for a long time, so I didn’t realize.

That the boss monster’s thin pupils were directed at me.

* * *

In an instant, Woo Sae-geon’s eyes returned.

From the intense energy that suddenly burst from beneath the island they were standing on, as if rewinding time, he moved his body first.

Woo Sae-geon kicked Sa Ye-ho, who was next to him, mercilessly hard. Sa Ye-ho flew away with an “Ahhh” and escaped the Myodusa’s attack range, and using that recoil, Woo Sae-geon charged toward Gyeon Gang-hyeon who was far away behind them.

Rumble—! In the moment when the ground split and everything visible was destroyed, Gyeon Gang-hyeon could only barely perceive unfamiliar clothing wrapping around him.

Immediately after, violent impact struck him. His consciousness flickered momentarily from the shock. The ground beneath his feet collapsed deeply.

In the void that had become an endless cliff, Gyeon Gang-hyeon’s two feet dangled helplessly.

Woo Sae-geon, gripping a protruding rock on the cliff, was looking down at him. With his upper body fully extended toward Gyeon Gang-hyeon hanging in midair, holding his arm.

Gyeon Gang-hyeon’s eyes met Woo Sae-geon’s as he regained consciousness. And at the same time, he felt that nothing was touching his dangling feet.

Goosebumps rose all over his body. It felt like hollow wind was seeping into his bones. The cold wind rushing up from below seemed to be sucking him into the endless precipice.

If Woo Sae-geon let go of this hand.

“Don’t look down.”

The person who had been looking straight into his eyes commanded with authority.

His knuckles dug strongly into Gyeon Gang-hyeon’s arm. He warned once before pulling.

“Put strength in your body. I’m going to pull you up.”

“Wait, ah…”

Gyeon Gang-hyeon couldn’t breathe properly and gasped. The trembling vibration was transmitted to Woo Sae-geon through the thin arm.

“I won’t drop you. Even if you fall, I’ll follow. If I fall with you, there will be a way to survive down there too.”

“That’s not it, that arm. Ah, ugh.”

Gyeon Gang-hyeon couldn’t put strength in his arm or relax it, not knowing what to do.

Looking again, the connection between Gyeon Gang-hyeon’s arm and shoulder that he was holding was somewhat unnatural.

“Is it dislocated?”

“Yes…”

Woo Sae-geon clicked his tongue softly. If he had pulled him up suddenly without warning, a terrible situation could have resulted.

[“…Gang-hyeon!”]

Come to think of it, noisy communication could be heard. Voices searching for them. No, they might actually only be looking for one person.

[“Hey, crazy. You’re going to jump down? Sae-geon hyung is with him, right? I saw it.”]

[“If both of you fell…?”]

[“I looked around once but they’re not here. I think we need to ride the dragon down there. Meanwhile, please find the Myodusa and keep it in check.”]

Gyeon Gang-hyeon gritted his teeth and gasped repeatedly. He should tell them he’s here, but the gravity pressing down on his whole body was too painful for his mouth to open.

“They don’t need to come. I’m with you.”

In Gyeon Gang-hyeon’s ears, Woo Sae-geon’s steady voice settled instead.

“Grab my arm with your other hand.”

Following Woo Sae-geon’s calm voice, Gyeon Gang-hyeon slowly raised his other arm that had been hanging limply. Swallowing the groan that was trying to escape, he desperately grabbed Woo Sae-geon’s arm.

After instructing him to hold his breath, Woo Sae-geon pulled him up sharply. While hanging from the cliff with one arm himself, he succeeded in getting Gyeon Gang-hyeon’s body onto one shoulder with just one pulling motion.

“Hang on directly.”

Muscles rippled like waves from arm to shoulder to back. Woo Sae-geon’s freed arms climbed up the cliff.

Due to being positioned at a right angle on those shoulders, Gyeon Gang-hyeon had to unwillingly feel all of that muscle movement directly.

But more uncomfortable than that was—

Why is this person muttering such things?

“Gyeon Gang-hyeon. If it had been the other way around and you were holding someone else, would you have been afraid of the cliff then too? …At that time, you would have said the same thing as me. ‘Even if we fall together, I’ll somehow manage it.'”

In front of the other successors, and furthermore in this stage situation that gets recorded, where ultimately all the citizens will hear…

Gyeon Gang-hyeon barely retorted.

“I am a show-off.”

The other person didn’t respond. Both were just saying what they wanted to say.

Even after climbing the cliff completely, Gyeon Gang-hyeon continued to be carried by Woo Sae-geon. Until Ja Hyeong-woo blocked their path.

“Gang-hyeon…!”

Ja Hyeong-woo approached by stepping on stones shattered and floating in the air. He carefully took Gyeon Gang-hyeon from Woo Sae-geon’s shoulder.
