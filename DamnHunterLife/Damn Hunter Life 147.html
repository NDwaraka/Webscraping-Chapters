The morning after Ja Hyeong-woo left, my condition was needlessly good and my mind was clear.

Training time was approaching, but I couldn’t turn away from the mirror for quite a while.

I was trying various mind control techniques so I could act nonchalant when Ja Hyeong-woo’s real form appeared. It wasn’t easy. My face was already burning even without seeing him in person.

‘Hah…’

Damn it, I was the leader. Unlike a month ago when I was just a single mola mola fish, as a leader I couldn’t skip group training.

At times like this, I desperately missed Yoon Sae-geon.

‘You’re my only leader. Come back and work. No re-awakening? Please re-awaken.’

I was insanely envious of that bastard who retired, put everything down, and was living well eating well while ignoring everything.

“Mungmung. I need your strength.”

Maybe because the mouse familiar was gone, I hugged the droopy Mungmung who’d been getting dejected lately.

I needed the broad, embracing heart of an earth attribute familiar. A heart deep and steady like earth, unshaken by anything.

{Hyeon has such great energy today! Amazing! Your expression is good too. A little red but no fever. But you need my strength? What should I do?}

“That… never mind.”

Our kid is especially bouncy today too.

When I gently pushed away Mungmung who was clinging to my chest, he suddenly stopped making a fuss and became quiet.

Then he suddenly said:

{A little sad but… it’s okay. I knew.}

He wiped around his eyes as if touched and stroked my cheek. …I definitely feel like I’ve seen this scene somewhere before.

So, like a mother crying while looking at the bride at a wedding…

It suddenly occurred to me that Mungmung couldn’t not know about yesterday’s incident, and what kind of state I must have been in flashed through my mind too.

Heat rushed to my ears and cheeks.

“What are you talking about! No!”

In the end, I only created misunderstandings with Mungmung, and arrived at the successor training ground with my head even more cluttered.

I was last. Even without me, everyone was warming up and drawing out their attributes by themselves.

“Gang-hyeon hyung, you’re here?”

At that moment, the massive blue form that had been stretching with his back turned slowly looked toward me.

My heart dropped with a thud. Time seemed to flow particularly slowly.

‘This isn’t anything else, it’s fear. Fear. I got predatory fear from a half-moon bear-sized bulk yesterday. If you’re a mouse, be mouse-like and stay smaller than me!’

When my eyes met Ja Hyeong-woo’s…

“Did you sleep well?”

This was the situation I encountered.

I almost dropped my jaw unconsciously, but barely held back seeing Hae Mi-jun’s fervent gaze looking at me.

I was barely relieved by the obedient answer that came back.

I opened the leader authority status window and called the kids together.

“Stop chatting. Let’s start training. Today…”

However, Ja Hyeong-woo’s gaze didn’t leave me throughout the training. He even wore what looked like an annoyingly happy smile.

‘Is that your idea of trying to be careful?’

Ja Hyeong-woo had regressed to his childhood.

That Han Hyeong-woo who would smile just from making eye contact with Cheon Gang-hyeon, would follow along and do whatever Cheon Gang-hyeon did while sending admiring looks, and would sometimes tremble as he barely held back his desire to pat that small head.

‘I’ve been getting irritated, venting my anger, and pushing him away for nearly a year because I was afraid this would happen… I’m screwed.’

During training and during the breaks in between, the other guys kept glancing at me and Ja Hyeong-woo. For the appeal of being ‘innocent,’ I deliberately put strength in my eyes and stiffened my neck.

Then I briefly made eye contact with Jin Seol-woon.

‘Ah.’

I found it hard to endure the gaze that maintained its heavy persistence from my side. Even though I felt uncomfortable doing this, I turned my head away first.

But where I turned my gaze, there was Ja Hyeong-woo again.

There’s a limit to being shameless. Do they think we’re having some secret romance? If they’re going to think that way, at least keep it ‘secret’!

After growling at each other until yesterday, if you’re suddenly smiling in one day, wouldn’t everyone think it’s strange?

‘But since he’s smiling, his face is somewhat bearable to look at…?’

I snapped back to my senses. It was a crazy thought. I pressed the chat buttons firmly while even feeling shame. I wanted to hit either my head or that bastard’s head.

Crash. It wasn’t overacting—I really tripped because my feet got tangled.

Ja Hyeong-woo might come running to help me up, but would I deliberately do such a thing?

Fortunately, Hae Mi-jun was nearby, so I was safe. I somehow avoided any physical contact with Ja Hyeong-woo.

For a while, I feel like just seeing that bastard’s palm would bring back that sensation.

‘Did he really eat something wrong? Does he have no shame? No dignity? That kind of talk comes out to me?’

How many dog-like things have I done and how many months have we spent like that? Does it make sense that all of that would disappear like melting snow?

‘Bastard with no substance.’

My heart has been off since earlier. It’s somewhat interfering with continuing the group training.

It’s not anxiety, but ambiguous emotions that aren’t quite irritation kept surging up. In an emotionally unstable state, I just bit my nails with my teeth.

Then I spotted Won Ha-ri right in front of me and flinched. Won Ha-ri tilted her head to the side and approached.

“Did Ja Hyeong-woo sunbae get your weakness?”

Yeah, a question like Won Ha-ri’s would be better.

Even slow-witted Yang Pyo came close while glancing at Ja Hyeong-woo. He whispered in my ear.

“Did you two make up?”

“Does this look like we’re on good terms now? It got worse!”

Even when I snapped angrily, Yang Pyo didn’t flinch. I’m not Yoon Sae-geon after all. With a rather displeased face, he said “Yes… well. Sorry about that.” and left.

Training ended somehow. The intensity and time were the same as usual, but my energy was completely drained.

The moment I gave the dismissal order. I saw it clearly.

Ja Hyeong-woo’s eyes flashing brightly, and a smile slowly blooming on his lips.

About 2 seconds later. A chase where only I was breathless began. I walked briskly through the training ground corridors.

He followed leisurely with large strides. My two steps equaled that bastard’s one step, which pissed me off.

“When we’re alone, I can talk to you, right? Let’s eat together.”

The steamed chicken from before briefly came to mind. Ja Hyeong-woo’s eating show that would aggressively boost my appetite too just from watching.

However, thinking about what those lips that would have spoons going into them did yesterday, I didn’t think I could eat at all.

“I don’t want to.”

“Tell me why you don’t want to.”

Something clicked in my heart. Yesterday’s promise hit me.

…I had promised to only tell the truth from now on. Even considering the sins I’d committed against him.

But the truth… no, I won’t say it! I can’t!

“Do you think I’ve cooled off now? Should I be calmly eating with you?”

Only then did Ja Hyeong-woo stop as if he’d fallen into a trap.

How many laps around the training ground had we made? We ended up back at the very first training room. Ja Hyeong-woo kept his distance and whispered softly.

“Sorry for making you breathless.”

…Is that an apology for right now?

Not an apology for yesterday’s incident—I wanted to seriously bang my head against the wall thinking up to that point.

“I’ll just see who you’re eating with and go.”

“What business is it of yours?”

“If you eat alone, it’ll be because of me. I feel sorry.”

Really, everything is sorry.

‘I’m fine eating alone, so why do I have to find a meal companion because of that bastard?’

Wondering if anyone might be in the training ground, I opened the door once.

I made eye contact again with Jin Seol-woon, who was sitting on a bench with his long legs stretched out while looking at his phone.

“…”

I turned my head pretending nothing happened.

Jin Seol-woon… won’t work.

The inner feelings Jin Seol-woon had suddenly revealed to me still confused me. Actually, looking back at everything up to now, it wasn’t completely out of nowhere, but my mind couldn’t understand it.

Since when? What was that guy lacking that made him…?

‘Even if Ja Hyeong-woo has connections from the past Cheon Gang-hyeon, Jin Seol-woon only has connections with me after I possessed this body. What the hell did I do?’

I plan not to see Jin Seol-woon in private settings from now on. Maybe knowing this, Jin Seol-woon doesn’t ask me to eat together anymore either.

Fortunately, there was another kid in the training ground besides Jin Seol-woon. I shouted loudly.

“Hae Mi-jun! Want to eat with just the two of us?”

“Huh?”

Hae Mi-jun, who had been organizing the swords used in training, widened his eyes at my call.

Then he immediately smiled brightly.

“Yes. I want to eat together! Come to our house.”

I happily gave my arm to the hand Hae Mi-jun extended. Hae Mi-jun bowed to Ja Hyeong-woo, then pulled me along without reading the atmosphere.

Has Hae Mi-jun finally regained his self-esteem? Did he accept that he’s not inferior to Ja Hyeong-woo?

While nodding approvingly, I had a somewhat unsettled feeling.

‘But just now… didn’t he greet rather carelessly? Could Hae Mi-jun do that?’

If I say his attitude seemed a bit different only toward Ja Hyeong-woo, would that just be my imagination?
