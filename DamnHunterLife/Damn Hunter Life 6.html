The bombshell statement from the Dog successor who had never shown his face.

This incident, which was no different from internal whistleblowing, created a controversy of considerable scale following ‘the chaos caused by the appearance of the first gate’, ‘Hunter School Stage 1 first strategy’, and ‘the first death among young hunters’.

It was certainly good that public opinion gained strength calling for banning indiscriminate content creation attempts from outsiders for students’ convenience. However, this didn’t stop there and spread into a situation where all disputes surrounding the special space called Hunter School caught fire.

What calmed the chaos was a single video directly delivered to the association by the Twelve Zodiac successors that same evening.

[Public]

Against a plain empty wall background, a black gat placed on a table appears on screen. Soon the video’s focus fixes on the face of the person sitting across from it.

The man wearing a light purple dopo with his hair in a topknot looked elegant like a Joseon Dynasty scholar at first glance. His deeply set eye sockets and protruding brow bones evoked the image of a banker working in finance. From any angle, he looked like someone who would suit calculators or keyboards rather than physical combat.

His identity was Woo Sae-geon, the ‘Ox’ successor, the eldest among successors and an irreplaceable leader.

If Ja Hyeong-woo was called the guardian deity of successors, Woo Sae-geon was called their war god.

[“Student Gyeon Gang-hyeon’s statement was not discussed with us successors.”]

Throughout the short announcement, his shadowed eyes didn’t blink.

[“We gratefully accept the positive interest toward Hunter School and will repay it with efforts to become even stronger.”]

Just as it seemed the video would end there, Woo Sae-geon raised his eyebrows high and took a few more seconds.

[“Gyeon Gang-hyeon.”]

His voice calling the name without warning was extremely sharp.

[“Do you think you’re qualified to say such things?”]

Gyeon Gang-hyeon, who watched the video to the end, smiled bitterly at home.

‘Oh my. He’s trying to kick me out.’

‘Before long, the Twelve Zodiac will need me.’
