Woo Sae-geon and the meeting room.

I wondered if other successors would be gathered too, but soon realized the Stage 30 feedback meeting would have already finished without me.

“Since there are no injured, we’ll do the feedback meeting right away. Rest comes after.”

“Ah, I’m too sleepy to endure it, so just do it without me……”

I escaped on my own feet, truly.

Being sick isn’t a crime, but that’s my situation and I am guilty. Woo Sae-geon must feel sorry that I’m such a weakling who collapses and breaks at every turn. If only my physical condition weren’t like this, he would have ground me to dust long ago.

‘Since the feedback meeting is over too…… thinking everyone would be resting, there’s a high probability Woo Sae-geon is alone in the meeting room.’

I briefly imagined what would follow. A scholar who looks like he wouldn’t bleed even if stabbed sits alone in a meeting room chair, I enter and stand before him. Starting with receiving his questioning……

My life would always have that human’s surveillance and supervision, receiving training pressure four times a day and getting eight messages asking if there’s progress in developing other attribute skills and having Woo Sae-geon personally grab my collar six times daily……

Just thinking about it makes me smile naturally. A smile of enlightenment.

‘I shouldn’t go.’

I instinctively sensed it. This is the crossroads of my life right now. I had to make the right choice.

That human Woo Sae-geon either doesn’t have a phone or has never taken out his phone even once. Not just in front of me, but in front of others too. Therefore, the means of contact with the leader was always through the status window.

I attempted communication with Woo Sae-geon while sitting at the dining table.

He did receive my contact, but.

[“Meeting room.”]

He immediately hung up.

I smiled bitterly while calling again. He answered this time too.

“I’m recuperating at home so I called.”

[“Recuperating?”]

An upturn at the end that clearly showed displeasure. The dark shadow cast over Woo Sae-geon’s eyes naturally came to mind.

[“Is this a call you’re making after organizing what you need to say to me?”]

All of Woo Sae-geon’s words to others are premised on a mindset that regards the other person as emotional and immature, unable to take care of themselves. And Woo Sae-geon doesn’t bother hiding this from the other person.

Appropriately suited to crush the spirits of those who are plentiful in Hunter School and actually young and immature.

Facing a person who had carved the intention to control others into all his actions, expressions, and language, I tried to keep my voice as light as possible.

“How much, what should I say?”

[“Affinity for other attributes.”]

“I’m not sure about that either…… it just happened somehow.”

Saying this would make Woo Sae-geon unpleasant even without facing him directly.

‘If you don’t want to hear kids rambling, why don’t you just ask them to write reports with introduction, body, and conclusion.’

But regarding this issue, I really have nothing to say either. After awakening, I can see others’ attributes – what am I supposed to do about it?

[“……Every day just brings more work. Just how much time do I have to pour into this bastard.”]

Woo Sae-geon muttered to himself. But I guarantee, Woo Sae-geon only mutters things he wants others to hear.

[“Also, I need to know to what level of intense activity your body can handle.”]

He must be talking about combat. He can’t risk putting me in a gate only to have me come out as a corpse.

So the conclusion seems to be, just teleport to the meeting room or training ground.

“As the leader saw, I have extremely limited stamina for fighting.”

Finding practical use in other attribute affinity, getting familiar with my body. Both require deep research.

But.

“I’ll research it myself.”

I have no intention of giving Woo Sae-geon my leash.

“It’s an ability only I have and only I understand, so what would change if the leader puts his head together with mine? And I don’t think showing everything to the leader is an obligation? Instead, if I discover something big, I’ll report it. Since you’re the leader who forms strategy teams.”

What this means is, I’m saying I won’t go under Woo Sae-geon but become a freelancer who occasionally receives stage and gate-related contracts from Woo Sae-geon.

“Leader, it’s true that you’ve done well with strategies even without me. Do you want to spend precious time on someone like me? Use it for bigger things.”

I expect Woo Sae-geon to understand the hidden meaning in my words.

If Woo Sae-geon still insists on managing and supervising me even after I’ve said this much—

This would mean Woo Sae-geon admits that the current successors’ military strength is insufficient and he needs me because of that.

‘In the original work, there was no successor named Gang-hyeon to begin with. Originally, I shouldn’t have been here.’

Woo Sae-geon’s lips faintly exhaled air.

[“Gang-hyeon.”]

“Yes.”

[“Don’t pull any tricks.”]

“Mm.”

Woo Sae-geon doesn’t bother decorating or beating around the bush with his words, so this part is comfortable. I listened to his words while grinning broadly, thinking of Woo Sae-geon being right in front of me.

[“Currently, using you brings more harm than benefit. As you said, I don’t care if you’re not there. I’ve never even thought about there being a Dog successor until now.”]

He truly sounded like he didn’t miss me one bit and had no regrets about it.

[“I might not send you out to stages and gates at all from now on. Do you know what happens then?”]

“What happens?”

[“There’ll be trouble with your retirement compensation.”]

At that moment, my spine went cold.

Since possessing this body, there hadn’t been many occasions to feel such sensation, so the chill was greater.

It doesn’t seem like a simple threat. This is the first thing Woo Sae-geon has seen through.

That no matter how unpredictably and capriciously I’ve been acting, ultimately I’m moving with post-retirement life as my goal.

Ah, so this becomes my weakness. My heart sank. The silence originating from me exceeded one second.

I carefully parted my lips.

“Retirement compensation is really important to me.”

There’s no point trying to evade here, it would just become more ridiculous. I judged it better to honestly acknowledge my weakness.

“Then just watch from afar. Whether I become someone worthy of compensation or not.”
