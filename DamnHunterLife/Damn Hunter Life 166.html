For someone who looked bewildered, Beom I-ryeong seemed to understand quite well what situation he was in right now.

Rather, I’m being more stupid right now. What should I do with this new successor who suddenly dropped from the sky?

I don’t really know, but for now I wrapped him in my clothes and brought him into the house.

‘If he’s fifteen, he needs to grow taller, so let’s put him to bed first. I’ll handle the work in the morning……’

It’s absolutely not procrastinating, and it would be awkward to wake everyone up noisily in the dawn when everyone was asleep. Moreover, the kid looked mentally hazy too, so I had no intention of making a fuss.

I gave Beom I-ryeong my bed. Beom I-ryeong obediently went under the covers and squirmed around to find a comfortable position.

‘It doesn’t seem like his voice has changed yet. He doesn’t look like a middle school second-year.’

No matter how I look at him, he seems younger, but since the minimum age for Hunter School is fifteen, he must be fifteen.

Beom I-ryeong, covered with the blanket, stared at me with wide-open eyes.

“Sleep well.”

I whispered softly, meaning for him to trust me even if he was confused. And as I turned around, my sleeve was suddenly grabbed.

“Isn’t this hyung-nim’s bed?”

Hyung-nim. Is my age at that level? Fifteen and twenty-three…… that’s about right. Yeah, at least I’m not an uncle.

“You use it.”

“Let’s sleep together. I’m fine with it.”

The voice speaking calmly was well-behaved. There was no sign of annoyance to be seen.

‘I should treat him gently. Even if it doesn’t suit my temperament.’

This is also a virtue of a leader. Though someone else never did that at all.

“Do that, then.”

I took out another blanket from the wooden cabinet and crawled onto the bed. We covered ourselves with separate blankets.

Beom I-ryeong lay beside me and stared intently at me. Before I knew it, he had turned his whole body toward me.

“Gyeon Gang-hyeon. I’ve heard a lot about you.”

Curious eyes focused intently on my profile. I couldn’t think of anything to say in response, so I just looked at the ceiling.

“……It must have been difficult.”

I received something like comfort from a fifteen-year-old.

Once again, I resented Park Yoon-seok. That uncle did things I didn’t even ask for.

“Sleep well. See you tomorrow.”

Beom I-ryeong completely closed his eyes. Soon his breathing became steady.

After Beom I-ryeong fell soundly asleep, I opened the status window that had been blinking since earlier and read the notification.

==

The ‘Tiger’ successor’s existence has been confirmed.

==

Yang among yin and yang, wood attribute. The third of the twelve zodiac. A promising dealer has appeared.

The next day passed in a flash without a moment to breathe.

I delivered the successor information to the association and received Beom I-ryeong’s personal information.

News was immediately reported outside as well. Inside, the ‘Tiger’ successor’s position was established.

“Hello, hello.”

Sa Ye-ho approached Beom I-ryeong, who was sitting on the office sofa with his knees together. He attempted small talk, being relatively well-behaved.

Beom I-ryeong just nodded shyly without confidence. His gaze was fixed only on me at the desk.

A middle school student who lived on the outskirts of Busan. He grew up looking at the sea. He doesn’t use dialect.

The middle school Beom I-ryeong attended was apparently very small. Perhaps because of that, there wasn’t a trace of posts on the internet saying “That’s my friend, he’s from our school.”

Articles about Beom I-ryeong were generally like this: Expectations that Beom I-ryeong would become a powerful weapon for ‘the lonely leader who rose above his past (I wish they wouldn’t call me this) Gyeon Gang-hyeon.’

‘Can’t they get by without mentioning me?’

Anyway, thanks to Beom I-ryeong, mentions of me in successor news are decreasing. It overlaps with when Won Ha-ri had just awakened.

Back then, I childishly cheered because I was buried thanks to her, but I shouldn’t do that now.

“It must be sudden and disastrous for you…… but still, I lived thanks to you. Thank you for coming.”

After the meeting with the association president, I casually said to Beom I-ryeong, who looked tired. I bent my knees and made eye contact.

“If I was helpful to hyung-nim, I’m happy too.”

He was a strange kid in many ways. His build was small like an elementary school student, but his posture was upright, unlike an ordinary fifteen-year-old. He seemed calmer than me.

I tried to think about what I was like at that age…… but I need to remember for that to work. Damn amnesiac possessor’s life.

Then let me recall the original Gang-hyeon at least.

……Hmm, let’s not recall that. It would be a comparison.

The moment cheeky little Gang-hyeon came to mind, I stopped thinking after 1 second. Instead, I mulled over the current situation with Beom I-ryeong joining.

The tenth team member who joined after Yoon Sae-geon left.

The recent burdensome stages. According to the hypothesis, the remaining stages are ’22’, ’11’, and ‘0’ stages.

‘Will Beom I-ryeong be able to fight in the final battle?’

I know he’s too young and inexperienced. It took Won Ha-ri a year to properly perform in stages too.

However, I also learned that if the situation is really unfavorable, you can’t be picky about people.

‘Still, it’s fortunate that there’s one more option to use.’

With Beom I-ryeong’s consent, I decided to start training right away while I was at it. At the same time, I’m going to have him meet the other successors one by one.

I’m a little worried, but I trust Myo Cho-hui’s public image.

After leaving the kid in her care, I hurriedly dealt with other tasks in the meantime.

I was so busy I could die. Just because a successor was added didn’t mean the existing workload decreased.

While returning from inspecting the training grounds, the 154th duty of the student council president, I met a familiar face across from the earth attribute gym.

I waved first. The naive-looking face was startled.

“Hello.”

It was Kwak Ji-hong, who had whined that he couldn’t do hunter work.

I must be uncomfortable for him. I thought Kwak Ji-hong would just bow his head and scurry away.

But surprisingly, that timid guy approached me closely. Then he hesitantly said:

“I, I started training. Even if I can’t enter actual combat. Since I’m the successor-nim’s first affiliated hunter…… I don’t want to embarrass you……”

Hadn’t the emotional pilgrimage of ordinary hunters ended yet? To think they even made this coward change. I stood with my mouth open for a while.

Kwak Ji-hong, who had been hesitantly raising his head, became dazed for a moment after checking my face.

“That’s enough. You’re more admirable than me.”

Even as I passed by, Kwak Ji-hong looked back at me several times. When our eyes met, he startled and looked straight ahead. He looked even more dazed.

I immediately headed to the training room where Myo Cho-hui and the young successor would be.

It seemed to be break time. Myo Cho-hui was standing some distance away, fiddling with her weapon, the glaive. Beom I-ryeong was sitting looking in a completely different direction. They looked very awkward.

‘Boys who don’t know Myo Cho-hui’s true nature get embarrassed when they see her. But this kid is strange.’

However, a fresh energy that hadn’t been there before was faintly flowing from Beom I-ryeong’s body.

I opened my eyes wide and approached with heavy steps.

“You manifested your attribute? In just one hour?”

Beom I-ryeong raised his head smoothly without being surprised. Myo Cho-hui nodded before Beom I-ryeong did.

‘That’s impossible. Then his attribute affinity is higher than even mine.’

Even if Myo Cho-hui was an incredibly excellent teacher, affinity was largely innate. It meant Beom I-ryeong was a genius.

As soon as Beom I-ryeong saw me, he left Myo Cho-hui behind and came running to me.

At his obviously delighted expression, I glanced at Myo Cho-hui. Perhaps?

“Why are you looking at me like that?”

“It’s nothing.”

Beom I-ryeong pulled my hand and placed it on top of his head. It looked like he wanted me to pet him.

At that moment, I flinched slightly.

‘What is this? It feels a bit different from Hae Mi-jun.’

He wasn’t even smiling. That expressionless face was peculiar.

Besides, isn’t this the age to be full of bravado? Would an ordinary middle school boy normally try to use an adult man’s hand like this……?

……Never mind. It must be because he relies on me.

“Why are you already manifesting attributes? You don’t need to do that.”

“I don’t need to do that?”

“No, I mean there’s no need to push yourself unnecessarily. Of course, the faster the better. It’s meal time, so let’s go eat.”

Beom I-ryeong and I left the training ground together. While going to the cafeteria, I asked:

“Is there any successor you’d like to meet and become close with? It’s uncomfortable because of our age difference. How about Hae Mi-jun?”

Beom I-ryeong didn’t seem very enthusiastic. The silent Beom I-ryeong grabbed my sleeve.

“I want to be with hyung-nim.”

If he had seen Hae Mi-jun even once on a broadcast, he wouldn’t react like this. Or does he just admire me more?

‘This kid has gone down the wrong path.’
