Damn Hunter Life Chapter 117

It’s nothing special of a question. I’ve heard it several times already.

It was a bit unexpected that even Jin Seol-woon, who thinks of Successors like office acquaintances, would ask this.

“I’m just weak.”

I don’t know how many times I’ve repeated the same explanation. At this point, my mouth just plays it back automatically.

“In all aspects of handling awakening abilities, if I use strength above a certain level, my insides get damaged or hurt. I’d probably be fine if I didn’t do anything. It’s because I do things that there’s a problem.”

I deliberately spoke as if it were no big deal while facing Jin Seol-woon. I was afraid the other person would pity me if I avoided his eyes. That kind of thing is absolutely disgusting.

“…I’m speculating that it’s aftereffects from failing awakening once, but honestly my memory of that time is hazy.”

Since no one except the original Gang-hyeon knows the detailed inside story of that incident, I’d be in trouble if asked in detail. It’s better to dodge by saying I forgot early on before being asked.

Jin Seol-woon didn’t probe further or even show a stirring reaction. He just quietly opened his mouth after listening to everything I said.

“Gang-hyeon-ssi is now an essential asset that can’t be missing from the Successors, and I know I’m not in a position to interfere. Ja Hyeong-woo-ssi must be taking good care of you…”

Was he trying to say something like whether I had to go out to stages?

Seeing how he shut his mouth, he didn’t cross the line. But if that’s the case, he shouldn’t have brought it up at all.

“Be careful. Be careful and careful again.”

The pleasant voice clung to my eardrums for a long time before falling away. At the same time, the troublemaker thoughts that had been ticking in my head quietly died down.

The faces of the other guys passed through my mind one by one.

Hae Mi-jun and Won Ha-ri, who are always worried and watching my complexion. Yang Pyo and Sa Ye-ho, who seem to tease but are secretly very worried.

‘These guys are really suffering from having the wrong teammate… Being distracted by worry for a sick person is absolutely not pleasant.’

And now even Jin Seol-woon.

‘And Ja Hyeong-woo…’

I soon gave up thinking about it altogether.

* * *

Unfortunately for Jin Seol-woon’s concerns, I was already on the roster for the very next stage.

The monster appearing this time was speculated to be earth attribute.

So they first deployed Myo Cho-hui, a wood attribute dealer who digs into earth, then filled in the remaining personnel.

====

Woo Sae-geon (Earth)

Ja Hyeong-woo (Water)

Myo Cho-hui (Wood)

Ma Cheon-ah (Fire)

Jin Seol-woon (Earth), Gyeon Gang-hyeon (Earth)

====

‘I seem like a side dish.’

It’s different from when Hae Mi-jun was attached at the very end before. He’s a useful healer, and I’m what.

Still, the fact that they included me means I’m at least better than nothing, right?

I’ve grown compared to the past when my very existence was a nuisance. Though he didn’t say it directly, Woo Sae-geon who saw the Four Divine Beasts skill seems to have judged that I have at least minimal value.

‘If not, killing me before retirement might have been part of Woo Sae-geon’s job. Whether socially killing me or assassinating me disguised as an accident…’

Anyone who saw that bastard’s eyes during the match would know. Those were eyes that looked like they’d really killed a person or two. How did such a dangerous person become a hero?

No. Still, it’s better than someone like me being a hero.

“Eek!”

It’s not like I had that thought just because I almost slipped and fell on the mud the moment I stepped beyond the stage entrance.

Fortunately, Jin Seol-woon who was next to me rescued me. I still staggered a bit after that, when suddenly something like a solid wall appeared behind my back.

‘What could be behind me is obvious without looking.’

I frowned and glanced back. Sure enough, there was Ja Hyeong-woo’s chest.

“I’ll…”

Ja Hyeong-woo tried to say something, but I snapped “Get lost to your position” and then linked arms with the arm Jin Seol-woon offered. It was unavoidable for proper walking.

Anyway, why is this place a tidal flat? Well, mud is earth too.

The higher the stage number, the more treacherous the terrain seems to become. I got suddenly angry because walking was uncomfortable. I got even more heated because everyone else was calmly walking while properly pulling out their feet that sank deep, while only I was swaying around.

“I’ve never once seen the dog enter a place gallantly.”

Ma Cheon-ah sneered with a face so arrogant it seemed to pierce the sky.

Then Myo Cho-hui, who had been on the east side, suddenly jumped forward. She looked around at the scenery before her with a puzzled expression, then spoke in a bewildered voice.

“There are no monsters.”

The monsters that were in the preliminary information, the cute toads, had all disappeared without a trace.

From Woo Sae-geon’s subtle expression, it seems he can’t feel any energy underground either.

I pointed to the blue water visible in the distance.

“No wonder it’s a tidal flat. There’s what looks like a sea… no, a big lake over there. They’re not hiding in that water, are they?”

Maybe the toads were water attribute, not earth attribute.

Before entering the stage, there had been conflicting opinions about whether toads were water or earth. So Woo Sae-geon had arranged our combination as a 4-attribute balanced type so we could prepare regardless of the attribute.

I opened the stage preliminary information again in the Status Window.

==

Appearing Monster: Water Ghost (B-rank)

Personnel Limit: 6 people

==

“What the hell is this…?”

Ma Cheon-ah, who checked after me, muttered in a stiffly hardened voice.

It seems the existing monsters drowned and water ghosts were created.

I had already found stages creepy given that monsters like Eodeukcseini appeared early on, and will-o’-the-wisps also appeared.

“This is the first time it’s changed in the middle.”

Jin Seol-woon echoed in a tense voice.

This is a variable even Woo Sae-geon couldn’t predict. I got furious instead.

“This is really fucking unfair! Monsters should just end when they die, why do they become ghosts!”

“Right! Where’s the common sense and conscience!”

Ma Cheon-ah chimed in with my words while fuming. Then our eyes met exactly.

I raised the corner of my mouth in a grin. Whether it was an unconscious reaction, Ma Cheon-ah belatedly lit fire in her eyes like a wild horse. She whipped her head away.

The leader’s silence didn’t last long. Woo Sae-geon soon calmly drew his twin swords and prepared to advance toward the water.

“Whatever monsters appear, what we have to do doesn’t change. Prepare for battle. Myo Cho-hui, if you find toad corpses, you collect them.”

Myo Cho-hui nodded resolutely with bright eyes.

Stage viewers would be enthusiastic about how brave she is, but that’s actually because Myo Cho-hui is someone who, like Woo Sae-geon, casually passes over gory scenes or blood-soaked monster corpses.

The lake was quiet without a single breeze. Under the blue surface, only clear ripples were visible.

But everyone knew. In reality, this lake was teeming with water ghosts trying to grab humans and drown them at any moment.

‘Someone might suddenly be grabbed and dragged into the water.’

Maybe right now they’re extending their arms to our feet, constantly waving their hands trying to grab at least our feet.

“How do we attack ghosts we can’t see?”

“What if Rabbit unni uses detection skills to tell us their locations?”

But we soon realized that Myo Cho-hui’s detection skill was useless here.

Seeing the energy of life is the ability of Myo Cho-hui, who possesses wood attribute.

But the opponents are already dead ghosts?

‘It would be the same even if Sa Ye-ho came. He finds enemies by detecting heat. Ghosts don’t have body temperature either.’

There’s no one in the Twelve Zodiac with the ability to detect ghosts…

That’s when an idea occurred to me.

“Just close your eyes!”

We can’t see the ghosts, but there are beings close to ghosts attached near us right now, right?

“If we switch to familiar vision and look, we’ll probably be able to see them!”

It seemed like it would work if each person rode their familiar and borrowed the familiar’s vision for long-range attacks.

I hadn’t tested it directly, but I was confident this would work.

I can feel Woo Sae-geon looking at me. Is he annoyed that I came up with a useful idea first? Serves him right.

“Then what about people without familiars?”

As soon as Myo Cho-hui’s words fell, everyone looked at Ja Hyeong-woo.

“…I’m a healer so it won’t be a big problem. I’ll stay far away and just heal. However, don’t go in recklessly to attack. You can’t count on my predictive healing this time.”

Woo Sae-geon put one sword back in the scabbard on his left side. Though twin swords were his specialty, he seemed to plan on using only one today.

Well, the number of swords didn’t matter. For casting earth attribute skills.

“Don’t let your body touch the water.”

Woo Sae-geon gave a warning. Since we couldn’t enter the water directly, everyone was going to consume large amounts of skills in this stage.

“Water ghosts can’t possibly be stronger than us, right? If we get caught, couldn’t we pull them out instead?”

“Your arrogance will kill you someday, Ma Cheon-ah.”

Woo Sae-geon coldly berated her. It was a statement without blood or tears.

Ma Cheon-ah seemed shocked and kept her gaze downcast for a while. However, since it’s not easy for someone with a天上天下唯我独尊 (heaven above, earth below, I alone am honored) personality to reform, she climbed onto Chollima and soon forgot what she’d just said.

“Wow… disgusting! They’re packed so densely.”

“But they look like people. I wonder if they weren’t made from dead toads.”

Jin Seol-woon also summoned the Dragon familiar and witnessed the swarm of water ghosts. Though Woo Sae-geon probably didn’t plan this far, the recent harsh familiar training had been helpful.

But something’s strange?

“Why doesn’t anyone care that I also can’t bring out a familiar and can’t do anything?”

Ma Cheon-ah openly makes laughing sounds. Even Jin Seol-woon seemed to be faintly smiling.

Even that bastard Ja Hyeong-woo gestured for me to come to the back where he was.

That bastard would feel comfortable with me quietly staying next to him. But do you think I’d do that?

‘I came all the way into a stage, so I should contribute too.’

I have my own thoughts.

Maybe now is the time to reveal this skill I have to everyone.

“Don’t be surprised if I suddenly disappear. I actually have a stealth skill and I’m going to use it.”

Because it was bothersome, I roughly notified the three who didn’t know about the stealth skill.

[<Boundary Between Life and Death (Earth Attribute)> skill is activated.]

Ah, now I can see the ghosts in the water too. There really are so many—

At that moment, my body stiffened completely.

All the ghosts were simultaneously poking their faces above the water surface and staring at me.
