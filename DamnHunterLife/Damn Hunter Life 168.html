Damn Hunter Life Chapter 168

Won Ha-ri lifted her beloved sword.

Then the large monkey also raised its mystical blade made of silver-white currents.

Pssss-swoosh. The monsters approached, bumping their straw limbs together.

Compared to the previous tigers and jangsan beom, they weren’t very threatening. Without much effort, swords cut through them, arrows pierced them, they burned easily in fire and quickly got soaked in water, making their movements sluggish.

But keeping in mind that carelessness is forbidden, we entered into full-scale strategy.

“Won Ha-ri, go wild on your own to your heart’s content. The rest of you support the sword dancer while coordinating according to orders.”

In front of the wood attribute monsters, I unleashed a successor who was a sharp blade by her very existence.

“Only use skills when I tell you to, and manage your stamina. It looks like it’ll be a prolonged battle due to the monsters’ mass offensive.”

“Yes!”

Hae Mi-jun answered energetically.

Behind him, I could see Myo Cho-hui and Ma Cheon-ah on one side, and Ja Hyeong-woo and Jin Seol-woon on the other.

The setting for facing monsters this time was a forest full of magnificent trees.

Wildflowers were in full bloom everywhere, and a rainbow even appeared over the picturesque waterfall. It was distracting in a different way.

‘This time the monsters are Jewung. And Maegu……’

Monsters with names meaning straw dolls swarmed in droves and surrounded us.

Jewung meant straw dolls made by humans. Depending on the maker’s intention, they could become cursed dolls, guardian dolls, or even protective dolls against misfortune.

‘Well, right now they’re just dolls that kill people.’

The monkey unleashed a terrifying momentum and swung its spirit sword. Each time it swept forward, straw monsters fell away in droves. Like an adult in front of children, the gap was very clear.

‘The fire demons last time were too harsh.’

Today the air is cool too, and the straw cuts cleanly. While carefully gauging the monsters’ characteristic dark energy without missing it, I shouted loudly toward the monkey familiar.

“Go Sungsong-ah! It’s your world!”

What is Sungsong now…… Won Ha-ri quietly grimaced. I deliberately giggled even louder.

‘Well, even if I die in 10 minutes, I’ll try to boost team morale first. Today Sa Ye-ho didn’t come with us either, so I have to work hard alone.’

Torn straw flew in all directions. Jin Seol-woon and Myo Cho-hui stood like wings on both sides, securing territory.

I managed Won Ha-ri dominating in front and Ma Cheon-ah spreading her wings in back while closely observing the surroundings.

Hundreds and thousands of Jewung were visible, but not a single strand of fox fur could be seen.

As often depicted in folklore, Maegu or the nine-tailed fox was said to have lived for a thousand years. If so, the monster that took that name would likely have equally high intelligence.

I don’t know for sure, but it might have intelligence like the fire demon stage boss from last time. This time conversation might be possible too.

Won Ha-ri was happily cutting through enemies in the front. She looked particularly good and energetic today.

<Sword Incarnation (Metal Attribute)>

She even drew out one of her metal attribute skills.

‘I’m glad Won Ha-ri at least is fine.’

On the other hand, I hadn’t even shot my bow thirty times yet but my arms were already getting tingly. I sighed inwardly.

I miss my physical condition from before I got sick. Actually, even then I was the weakest of the weak, but to think it could get even worse from there.

I have to conserve even a few arrows. Knowing my condition, Ja Hyeong-woo seriously suggested leaving me out of the 22nd stage. I snapped at him saying not to talk nonsense.

Everyone seemed to have developed trauma instead of me. They kept glancing at me and managing things so that not even a piece of straw would graze me.

Jin Seol-woon was attacking somewhat aggressively for a while, but then went back to being a defensive tanker, wrapping us in a circle with his dragon.

Ma Cheon-ah wasn’t showing off but was faithfully blocking in front of me, so that says it all.

While clearing up the straw dolls rushing in like clouds, something strange caught my eye.

One Jewung that was somehow wearing something like white cloth, had an unusually small head and long legs proportion, and seemed to be swaying somewhat.

……The moment Won Ha-ri’s sword was about to touch it, I shouted loudly.

“Wait!”

I shot an arrow and barely deflected the sword. That Jewung tumbled among the other monsters.

I aged ten years. Feeling cold sweat running down my spine, I let out a sigh of relief.

‘That was almost fucked.’

The Jewung here were cursed dolls.

“That thing just now looked exactly like me with its stupid appearance.”

Understanding my words one by one, everyone’s faces turned pale.

They seemed to imagine the consequences if Won Ha-ri’s sword had struck that Jewung directly. Won Ha-ri almost dropped her sword.

“Hey, can a swordsman drop their sword?”

I quickly mocked her jokingly.

As expected, the monsters here weren’t completely easy either.

If there were monsters among them that had to be kept alive, Won Ha-ri would become noticeably timid.

‘Won Ha-ri gets too nervous about potentially hurting others. Though she actually does hurt people.’

“There are Jewung mixed in that were made copying us. They seem to be cursed dolls. If the monster gets hurt, that person will get hurt the same way. Avoid them and attack!”

The cursed dolls quickly emerged as a considerable nuisance.

Protecting specific Jewung while selectively defeating only the others wasn’t an easy task. No matter how much group training we do to avoid team-killing colleagues normally, it’s different when they’re enemies.

In other words, would those Jewung modeled after us just stay still?

Until now, we had been killing Jewung before they could attack us. Now that Jewung appeared that we couldn’t do that to, the combat advantages that Jewung possessed finally became visible.

First, though they were made of straw, when they swung their arms widely, it delivered blows like iron clubs. And their ends were quite sharp, so when stabbing, they were like fencing swords.

“Ack! Dragon oppa stabbed me!”

Among the special Jewung, one with white cloth feathers tried to poke Ma Cheon-ah with its arm. Ma Cheon-ah was making a fuss even though it didn’t even touch her.

Jin Seol-woon, who had been maintaining distance from other successors, just slightly furrowed his brow and passed it by instead of counterattacking.

‘There’s going to be an accident at this rate.’

There are limits to fighting while ignoring and avoiding them. The kids’ movement patterns keep getting tangled.

I need to do something about those Jewung.

‘There’s nothing useful among Ja Hyeong-woo’s curse skills. Should I use the dragon familiar to send them far away somewhere? But what if Jin Seol-woon or the Jewung get hurt in the process?’

At this moment, my eyes met with Hae Mi-jun who was beside me. As if reading my thoughts, our gazes exchanged, and Hae Mi-jun spoke first.

“Gang-hyeon hyung, perhaps I could……?”

Suddenly the not-so-distant past flashed through my mind.

It was right after I got up from the sickbed and Gang-hyeon’s past became known to the world.

It wasn’t just me whose abilities improved, and it wasn’t just Won Ha-ri who succeeded in familiar control.

Hae Mi-jun also achieved unexpected results.

But it wasn’t miraculous healing skills like Ja Hyeong-woo’s, or support skills like anesthesia skills.

<Clear Blue Winter (Water Attribute)>

It was an attack skill.

When I first saw that skill, I—.

“What did you do?”

I scolded Hae Mi-jun.

“Attack skills aren’t essential. You’re a healer. You should treasure your body the most. You don’t need to do things like this!”

I was perplexed and unconsciously pressed him, then belatedly looked at Hae Mi-jun.

But Hae Mi-jun held his head up straight and acted quite confidently.

“I wanted to become someone trustworthy to Gang-hyeon hyung. Like Hyeong-woo hyung. I’m sorry for surprising you……”

He seemed to be looking ahead to becoming the sole healer for a while after Ja Hyeong-woo retires next year.

If Ja Hyeong-woo uses debuff skills or curse skills for attacks, Hae Mi-jun can make more full-scale attacks than that.

Ice.

In a way, it’s the power closest to the essence of water attribute.

“Catch the strange Jewung one by one and freeze them. Don’t freeze them too solid. Make it feel like you’re imprisoning them by leaving the inside of the ice empty. Can you do it?”

“Yes, I’ll try!”

When Hae Mi-jun joined the attack side, the cursed Jewung were quickly subdued. The formation stabilized again.

The number of monsters decreased rapidly, and the last Jewung completely fell.

It was right then that the boss monster spread an invisible presence and influenced the stage somehow.

“……!”

Suddenly all the successors disappeared from in front of me.

I turned on communication first and quickly observed my surroundings. I held my bow and took a defensive stance.

The fortunate thing was that group communication wasn’t cut off. Everyone quickly responded.

[“What is this!”]

[“I don’t know where this is!”]

The young ones were surprised and flustered.

[“Gang-hyeon, are you okay?”]

[“The boss monster seems to have scattered us apart. We need a way to regroup.”]

On the other hand, Ja Hyeong-woo quickly asked about my safety first. Myo Cho-hui grasped the situation faster than I did.

Only after confirming that all six people except me were fine could I see my situation.

‘Right now I’m alone. Maybe one-on-one with the boss monster.’

Tension immediately rose.
