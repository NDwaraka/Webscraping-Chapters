Damn Hunter Life Chapter 143

Maybe it’s because I suffered severe mental trauma and came crawling into my den like an injured animal seeking shelter.

I felt uneasy about Jin Seol-woon being in my space.

Also the fact that Jin Seol-woon had been waiting for me in the dark room with blackout curtains drawn, without even turning on the lights, like a predator stalking its prey.

Why did he come?

He’s definitely not the type to cross lines like this. If anything, I would be the one crossing them.

“I’m sorry for coming in without permission. But Gang-hyeon-ssi also has a record of doing this.”

Even the person speaking sounded as if such things weren’t important, his voice completely flat. Or perhaps his mind was focused on other thoughts.

Setting aside doing something he’d never done before, something was different. His face half-buried in the shadows was mysterious.

“I haven’t been here long. I just teleported to Gang-hyeon-ssi.”

Only then did I notice the notification floating in my status window.

The fact that I couldn’t even see this meant my mind was completely elsewhere…

Belatedly, I remembered my face that would still be bright red. I quietly slipped out through the doorway.

“I’ll just go to the bathroom for a moment.”

Looking in the mirror, my face was even more of a sight. I encountered a face that should absolutely never be shown to others.

This is why Ja Hyeong-woo did that… that thing and stared at me intently.

There was no more blood left to rush to my head. What on earth must Jin Seol-woon have thought seeing this?

‘Or maybe not. Maybe he’d think it’s because I’m sick. Come to think of it, why didn’t he ask if I’m okay?’

As soon as I washed my face and came out, Jin Seol-woon peeled his back off the wall as if he’d been waiting.

“I have something to say. It’s about Gang-hyeon-ssi’s body. I heard and found out some things.”

Something I don’t even know exactly myself, what did he hear from whom to find out?

“Are you ready to listen?”

Maybe because I felt anxious due to Jin Seol-woon’s presence, I didn’t feel like sitting down. I stood leaning against the opposite wall just like Jin Seol-woon.

My legs didn’t hurt or feel weak. Unlike before I fell asleep. While I couldn’t pay attention to that point right now, I watched the movement of Jin Seol-woon’s lips.

“Gang-hyeon-ssi’s physical condition can’t be fixed with just a healer’s healing.”

Suddenly something like a terminal illness diagnosis came at me.

“Gang-hyeon-ssi’s dog belongs to ‘yang.’ So you created skills by receiving yin-type attribute energy to achieve harmony. But Gang-hyeon-ssi is absolutely lacking in that yang energy. You have something like a deficiency syndrome.”

Jin Seol-woon wasn’t speaking to me in a reproachful way asking why I, the person involved, didn’t know. Nevertheless, I felt ashamed of myself. At the same time, I was flabbergasted.

“And because of that, every time you used skills, Gang-hyeon-ssi was robbed of the yang energy you needed to survive. That’s why your body was getting worse. Seeming to harbor yin energy, seeming to have weak stamina…”

My head spun from the string of words.

Then how had I been alive until now? Without being aware of such crucial information…?

As if I already knew the answer to that question… I felt my lips, slightly more swollen than usual.

A cool voice awakened my mind that was about to go blank. Jin Seol-woon didn’t let me think of other things.

“Still, there’s a way to endure it. You can make up for it by having someone else give you yang energy.”

Jin Seol-woon suddenly rolled up his shirt sleeves one by one. As if he’d gotten hot from rising temperature. I flinched at the sight of his snow-white arms moving.

“I want to help you.”

At neither slow nor fast speed, long legs crossed the middle of the room.

Soon my right side was blocked by a solid bare forearm.

Jin Seol-woon stood in front of me with his hand against the wall. My vision was restricted, and I could clearly see his sharply carved jaw, trained neckline, and the Adam’s apple that protruded and cast shadows. It was a distance so close it would be hard to forget.

Even though Jin Seol-woon wasn’t touching any part of my body, my arms wouldn’t move as if I were tied up. Words wouldn’t come out either.

“Gang-hyeon-ssi.”

A voice with a slightly trembling end rang right next to my cheek.

A faint scent of cologne wafted over. Also Jin Seol-woon’s body scent that was hard to smell elsewhere, only coming from his house.

As my sense of smell was stimulated, my throat that had been blocked as if paralyzed opened right up.

“Don’t.”

His lips stopped without coming any closer.

It was a terribly hoarse voice, but as if that one word contained powerful force, Jin Seol-woon couldn’t move.

“Am I not allowed?”

Eyes like a perfect creation held a lyrical color. Though it seemed like deliberately flowing sensuality that made me feel repulsed, dry saliva went down my throat.

My head became dizzy again.

“…You’re an idol.”

“Idols can’t do this?”

A slightly high-pitched and urgent, questioning tone followed without any gap.

I hesitated briefly on how to respond, but I, as a human being, ultimately only knew the most concise and intuitive way to speak.

“It would be the same even if you weren’t an idol.”

A silence like having strongly struck the other person fell with a thud.

Maybe because there were many heart-dropping incidents today, I seemed to hear even the sound of the other person’s heart stopping.

Jin Seol-woon’s eyes and the corners of his mouth distorted simultaneously. I felt like something was collapsing inside my heart. It was hard to watch, so I also squinted my eyes.

Even as his pupils shook here and there from shock, he had enough courtesy to remove his arm from me and step back.

“Are you going to Ja Hyeong-woo-ssi?”

Jin Seol-woon rapidly spat out the words.

I’m shameless, but I think I also felt a bit betrayed.

“You said you had no other feelings for me.”

I’m annoyed from the confusion. Like when I faced Agni.

“Do you like Ja Hyeong-woo-ssi?”

“Don’t pry into everything. I don’t feel any reason to answer.”

That wasn’t the Jin Seol-woon I knew. Neither the original Jin Seol-woon nor the TV Jin Seol-woon.

He had completely lost his composure. Emotions preceded reason, his mind was a mess, and what he was thinking showed on his expression without filter.

Just like me right now.

An expression mixing dissatisfaction and regret with himself, as if someone who’d lived excellently his whole life couldn’t accept this one moment.

Suddenly I thought I’d never looked at Jin Seol-woon’s face in such detail before. At a glance, he’s a handsome face, so what was this feeling like?

When my thoughts reached that point, the thorns that had been growing sharp enough to prick even myself instantly subsided. The strength drained out of me.

“Sorry. My tone was harsh just now.”

I lowered my tail first. Even prepared for that difficult question to surface again. Because this is right.

Jin Seol-woon, who had walked to stand against the wall, inhaled and exhaled several times.

After a short time passed and he turned his head, Jin Seol-woon’s eyes had become calm again like they usually were.

“No, I’m sorry. I didn’t know what to do either… So, that is. My emotions got ahead of me. I was also sudden about it. The things I learned today.”

Though he was still rambling.

“He doesn’t like me that way. I don’t… either.”

For some reason, Jin Seol-woon stared at me for a long time as if he couldn’t believe it at all. However, he didn’t pursue anything further.

“I’ll go. Gang-hyeon-ssi is sick. Instead, later, please make time for me separately. …I’m saying give me a proper chance to speak too.”

Though the inside of his pupils still couldn’t shake off their turbidity, by the time he finished speaking, the Jin Seol-woon I knew had returned a little.

I obediently nodded.

To soothe the shock, I ate a meal and took a 30-minute bath in the bathroom.

Certainly, compared to before I dozed off in the training room, my body had clearly improved. I didn’t get dizzy even staying in hot water for a long time.

“…”

Having stopped all thoughts, I entered work-from-home mode. I mechanically read and organized school documents. I received the documents from Sa Ye-ho who was in the office.

During this time, my phone kept ringing.

[Cooperate with me by today. First get your body completely better, then get angry.]

[I’ll wait.]

I skimmed the honest texts with periods faithfully placed at the end of every sentence. I turned my eyes away pretending not to see and just read through documents continuously.

Absentmindedly, I swept my lower lip with my tongue, then suddenly heat rushed to my face. Hissing, I quickly grabbed my phone.

[Don’t let me see you]

Then more than ten texts came flooding in saying I needed more treatment, sorry, and so on.

I should have told him to shut up too.

‘But if what Jin Seol-woon said is true, and I’ll die if Ja Hyeong-woo doesn’t do that… thing.’

I should have looked into it from when fusion skills required physical contact. It’s lamentable that I only realized it now.

What remained for me as the price was a frail body that urgently needed treatment and a life debt owed to Ja Hyeong-woo…

…If I have to keep kissing someone, in the end I only have one choice.

If I had to do such things, there was ultimately only one person within the range I could endure.

I picked up my phone. With a feeling equivalent to jumping off a cliff, I pressed three keys firmly. And sent it to Ja Hyeong-woo.
