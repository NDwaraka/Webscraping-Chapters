Damn Hunter Life Chapter 177

* * *

Blue waves peeled away the pain layer by layer.

Yoon Sae-geon opened his eyes and took in the dim warehouse scenery. He was still lying face down with his head turned to the left.

‘Am I seeing things?’

For a moment he felt the illusion that time had rewound. To a not-so-distant past.

Closest to him, Ja Hyeong-woo, who had finished healing, withdrew his hand and turned his head before their eyes could meet.

If it had only been Ja Hyeong-woo, he would have thought it was quite an unpleasant illusion. The problem was those 8 people crowding over there.

They didn’t care at all that Beom I-ryeong looked exactly like a human. They skillfully surrounded him so there was no escape route and threw multiple weapons as if Beom I-ryeong were a target board. They looked overheated beyond normal, making Yoon Sae-geon frown.

But even so, hardly any skills were coming out.

They must be saving them for the stage. On the leader’s orders.

Sa Ye-ho trampled on Beom I-ryeong’s face while making eye contact with Yoon Sae-geon.

“List out one by one how hyung got hurt and why he got hurt.”

Yoon Sae-geon’s gaze touched the gasping Beom I-ryeong. Beom I-ryeong had a large needle stuck in the middle of his waist and couldn’t move his lower body.

“Let me hear you say it.”

Yoon Sae-geon’s gaze moved to Sa Ye-ho’s front covered in blood and the hand casually holding a reddened dagger. His mouth slowly opened.

“Motorcycle inexperienced driving.”

“…Huh?”

A stupid light instantly flowed across Sa Ye-ho’s fierce face.

While the successors were driving the fake tiger into a corner to hunt it, Gyeon Gang-hyeon wasn’t joining in and was watching from behind.

The vividly clear truth was shaking his head.

‘I’m the same existence as spirit monsters.’

So that’s why ghosts keep acting familiar, why I can touch soul stones, and why Beom I-ryeong felt kinship and kept bothering me.

—Hyeon-ah. What should I do with you.

If this was the reason why in some future of the original work, Ja Hyeong-woo said meaningful words with a face that seemed confused?

Future Ja Hyeong-woo definitely met dead Gyeon Gang-hyeon.

Then is now different from the original work?

‘Because I didn’t die, because I tried hard, because I opened all my power—is it different from that original work back then?’

He soon shook his head and focused on the situation. While Gyeon Gang-hyeon had stopped giving orders, the scene before his eyes was becoming more and more excessive suppression.

Ghosts without magic stones and no vital points just had to be beaten all over to weaken them. Scattering their energy to make them disappear.

So the attacks the successors were making on Beom I-ryeong felt similar. However, Beom I-ryeong had the annoying trick of gathering his energy back together even after it was all scattered, perhaps due to his persistent vitality.

“Ja Hyeong-woo, let’s try your method. Take out the gourd.”

To prevent the annoying resurrection, Gyeon Gang-hyeon made him use Ja Hyeong-woo’s skill that he’d used before.

<Bottle That Contained Medicinal Wine (Water Attribute)>

Beom I-ryeong, whose form was ruined from being dug up to death, couldn’t resist Ja Hyeong-woo’s skill. The gourd sucked in the tattered Beom I-ryeong.

Crash! The gourd shattered to pieces in Ja Hyeong-woo’s hand.

When the surroundings became quiet, a sigh came out.

Now he’s really dead. Let’s never see him again.

When everything was over and they looked around the old warehouse, the headcount was ’10 people.’

Somehow it felt like they’d become complete. Gyeon Gang-hyeon grinned as he looked back at Yoon Sae-geon.

“How do you feel about seeing us again?”

The young successors still seemed unable to forget the old Woo Sae-geon. They had stiffened and were avoiding his gaze. Only Gyeon Gang-hyeon twisted his lips and teased.

Yoon Sae-geon expressionlessly adjusted his clothes with light touches.

“Let’s pretend we didn’t see each other.”

What a boring person. Gyeon Gang-hyeon told Sa Ye-ho to escort Yoon Sae-geon home.

“The school boundary is broken anyway… Jump well so you don’t get caught on surveillance cameras or anything. Got it? If Yoon Sae-geon is slow, carry him or something.”

Yoon Sae-geon was displeased and Sa Ye-ho was delighted with giggles.

A little later, a text from Yoon Sae-geon flew to Gyeon Gang-hyeon’s phone.

[Explain later how you got out of school]

* * *

Today has been a very long day. Even after Sa Ye-ho returned to school, the successors didn’t disperse to their respective homes but stayed gathered.

“Now explain.”

Jin Seol-woon urged me from the front.

Why are there so many people telling me to explain? I grumbled only internally.

‘Everyone was shocked when I first said Beom I-ryeong was fake too.’

Beom I-ryeong… the Jangsan Tiger that they already knew had been killed twice—how was it alive again? What did the things it said mean?

I told them about what had happened so far.

“Beom I-ryeong was trying to remove me from the Twelve Zodiac. Or else eliminate Ja Hyeong-woo who’s treating me.”

This much was the part everyone knew from hearing about it.

But regarding the detailed inside story, until now only I and Ja Hyeong-woo knew, keeping it secret from others.

“What’s strange is that even though killing me would be faster, it said to ‘follow.’ It cornered me but didn’t kill me. It has another purpose.”

Since even I don’t know everything perfectly, the story was vague. Somehow when it ended, the team members had gathered in a tighter circle around me.

“Are the monsters trying to steal your power?”

Myo Cho-hui threw out a hypothesis with a deeply thoughtful face.

“I’m also assuming something like that for now.”

Because Myo Cho-hui’s guess was more plausible than my dangerous hypothesis, I roughly agreed.

Yang Pyo, Won Ha-ri, and Hae Mi-jun had noticeably worried expressions. Won Ha-ri gripped my hand tightly.

“Gang-hyeon oppa shouldn’t enter stages.”

“But what if that’s what the monster intends?”

A sharp voice popped out without me realizing. The little ones got a bit scared at that. I went “oops” and gently redirected my words.

“My role can’t be replaced anyway. The situation won’t improve just because I drop out of the clearing team.”

Beom I-ryeong wanted me to break the system and become an alien existence.

Like the status window.

‘Then does that mean they’re all in cahoots?’

I had no family or friends, so there was no one in Korea worth taking hostage. But that Jangsan Tiger bastard even found Yoon Sae-geon.

As a result, the boundary separating Hunter School, Korea’s first gate, from the outside was destroyed. I checked, but it didn’t seem like it would reattach.

‘What the hell did I do?’

While rummaging through the status window because the aftermath was overwhelming, I suddenly remembered.

‘It’s not just successors who are trapped in school.’

And at that moment, the status window blinked. A notification from just a few seconds ago popped up precariously.

==

/Departure confirmed.

/Departure confirmed.

/Departure confirmed.

==

Students were carrying out mass escapes, wondering ‘what happened?’ Once the fence collapsed, everyone went “waaaaah” and escaped beyond it.

Uh… huh? While staring dumbfounded at only the status window, I was belatedly shocked.

“Come baaack!”

I screamed like calling runaway dogs.

* * *

Hunters can go outside Hunter School. They can even return home.

What all students and their families had so desperately wished for since Hunter School’s early days had magically come true.

But if that happens overnight, and when everyone has already adapted to gate dispatches centered around the school?

‘This is total chaos.’

After forcibly summoning all students back to school, I put up a bullet-fast general announcement. I wrote it hastily but roughly included all the important content.

‘Succeeded in releasing the system’s constraints, but need some time for stabilization, so I’ll prepare for 3 days then send you out in shifts.’

After sending all the announcements, only then did a sigh rush out. It feels like somehow hastily plugging a dam that was about to burst and overflow.

But the work isn’t finished yet. An external announcement is also needed.

‘And the announcement has to be made through the Association.’

Multiple pairs of eyes kept watching me struggling while looking at empty air.

I grinned at my team members.

“Let’s go have some fun somewhere.”

The Korean Hunter Association building I was seeing in person for the first time was magnificent. It looked even cooler at night.

Seeing it in a situation where we were about to barge in recklessly, it was even more splendid.

I brought my left arm ‘Chattering Mouth That Never Rests’ Sa Ye-ho and my right arm ‘Scariest-Looking Healer in the World’ Ja Hyeong-woo and kicked open the Association President’s office door.

Kim Jae-moon spat out the tea he was drinking. He stared at us with bulging eyes as if he’d seen a ghost.

I slapped my hand on Kim Jae-moon’s frozen desk. I grinned and opened my mouth.

“Well, long time no see. Have you been well? Oh my, I didn’t know your complexion was this bad in person.”

Sa Ye-ho didn’t even need to add atmosphere from the side. I swaggered and made Kim Jae-moon’s face turn deathly pale.

Yang Pyo quietly said ‘like gangsters’ from behind. However, since he was also contributing greatly to looking like a gangster with his own bulk, he had no right to say such things.

To conclude, Kim Jae-moon shriveled up without making a peep as soon as he saw the successors in person. He had to grant all our demands. While summoning the entire Association staff this night.

‘It wasn’t intentional but… did I succeed in crushing Kim Jae-moon ajeossi’s spirit with this?’

Moreover, I secured tremendous popular support from the students through this incident.

I didn’t particularly announce it as something I did, but when I came to my senses, it was already known among hunters through word of mouth. Now students practically worshipped me as a god.

* * *

Stage 11 was really imminent.

A few days before entering the big stage, I gave the successors unprecedented special treatment with the special ability I’d recently gained.

I gave them a day off. I sent the successors back so they could spend time with their families.

The Twelve Zodiac that had been gathered in one place temporarily returned to their original birthplaces. As someone’s son, daughter, or friend.

But I had nowhere to go.

I had no family and no acquaintances.

Even after receiving a precious day off, the other successors kept glancing at me and hesitating. They were concerned that I was staying at school with nowhere to go. Since they were being considerate and not going, I felt more awkward.

Then Ja Hyeong-woo stepped forward.

“I’m not going either. I’m not particularly close with my guardian. I don’t really want to go meet them.”

Then everyone heard that Ja Hyeong-woo was staying too and just left.

‘These bastards.’

As Sa Ye-ho, Ma Cheon-ah, and others found out about our relationship one by one, now it seems like all the successors have caught on. I thought I was hiding it…

Some aren’t interested, some occasionally look with strange eyes but don’t mention it, some giggle. These damn guys without a single prejudice.

Not knowing what I was thinking inside, in the school with just the two of us, Ja Hyeong-woo followed around behind me like a big dog.

I turned my head sharply. Ja Hyeong-woo wasn’t even surprised and met my gaze with sparkling eyes.

‘It’s all because of this bastard.’

I hit his solar plexus with my elbow with a thud. This guy’s solar plexus was also hard, so I hurt more.

Though there was no way it hurt, Ja Hyeong-woo bent his body. I wondered why, then saw he was checking if my elbow was bruised.
