Damn Hunter Life Chapter 137

As the training came to an end, the video length also reached its end. It was exactly the 20 minutes Gyeon Gang-hyeon had suggested.

Even considering that the successors moved quickly because they were afraid he might cough up blood, the growth between the start and end of training was as excellent as the previous leader. The Gyeon Gang-hyeon in the video was sufficient to convince anyone.

Just as Ja Hyeong-woo had said.

As soon as he declared break time, Gyeon Gang-hyeon’s expression relaxed as if by magic. He returned to his usual face and made a commotion, patting Yang Pyo’s shoulder.

“Alright, alright. This is much better than yesterday.”

“How serious was it yesterday…”

“It was pretty bad. To the point where I wondered if things were really difficult lately?”

“Leader looks the most exhausted though.”

“Yeah, I’m exhausted. Someone hug me.”

When Gyeon Gang-hyeon, who had set the mood, looked to the side, Hae Mi-jun, who followed him around like a tail these days, was right nearby. Hae Mi-jun shyly extended his arms slightly. Gyeon Gang-hyeon leaned against Hae Mi-jun and relaxed comfortably.

“I’ll tell you what I saw, so listen. Sa Ye-ho, you first.”

The new leader wasn’t particularly generous with praise either, but at least his feedback was gentler compared to the previous leader. And his ability to pinpoint detailed aspects was outstanding.

He properly observed the movements that occurred when several people tangled simultaneously, and even caught new minor habits like which person looked where first in an instant. He suggested more efficient methods for using non-earth attribute skills as well.

And the threat he always added at the end.

“If you don’t fix what I told you, you’re dead.”

“Gasp…”

“I’m the one who’ll be dead.”

“Hey hey, let’s do this properly. Every time you make a mistake, you’re taking 1 year off our leader’s lifespan.”

The video ended with the successors laughing like any students as they left the training ground. They approached the camera by the door.

Ja Hyeong-woo, who was at the doorway, reached out his hand the moment Hae Mi-jun and Gyeon Gang-hyeon passed by. He tried to naturally steal and embrace Gyeon Gang-hyeon. His grip lightly embraced Gyeon Gang-hyeon’s back and pulled.

“…?”

The thin body didn’t follow and stayed in place. Not because of the person himself, but because the person holding him gripped tightly.

When Ja Hyeong-woo looked, Hae Mi-jun bowed his head deeply. As if avoiding his gaze.

Gyeon Gang-hyeon, who realized the situation a beat late, frowned slightly.

“What are you doing? Just come out.”

Due to being hidden by the wide white sleeves, Hae Mi-jun’s hands weren’t captured by the camera. Ja Hyeong-woo, who had nothing to argue back with, just followed out empty-handed as the scene ended.

[“…It was filmed like this. Would you like me to edit it if you want?”]

Some time later, Gyeon Gang-hyeon checked the latter part filmed at the end. Ja Hyeong-woo’s appearance that looked like he’d been rejected to anyone watching.

PD Hong Jeong-hyeok, while editing the video, contacted Gyeon Gang-hyeon directly saying he’d received it through Sa Ye-ho.

For Gyeon Gang-hyeon, it was somewhat puzzling why he was asking him this specifically. Had Ja Hyeong-woo been filmed looking too pitiful? Or was he worried Gyeon Gang-hyeon would get cursed at by people?

‘But why is this person being nice to me? Because I’m the leader?’

“You don’t need to cut it. Just leave it.”

Gyeon Gang-hyeon’s decision to leave that scene had two reasons. First, he wanted to deliberately show people himself responding curtly to reduce the rumors about him and Ja Hyeong-woo circulating in the community.

Second, he had the ulterior motive of inducing sympathetic public opinion for Ja Hyeong-woo, who would be slandered somewhere as a ‘homo’ due to those rumors.

‘…Sigh.’

After glancing at the tanned face in the frozen screen, he quickly averted his eyes as if uncomfortable. He sighed ten times.

Jin Seol-woon watched the uploaded training video to the end while checking the chat that had come up during the initial release.

‘Fuck now I feel sorry for him’

‘Just date already’

‘At that level gender doesn’t seem to be the issue’

The chats were uncomfortable to look at. If he could, he’d want to delete them all. So neither he nor anyone else could see them.

Especially so Gang-hyeon-ssi wouldn’t see them.

Even though he was at home, he looked into the air as if Ja Hyeong-woo was standing at a distance.

‘Is that person’s feelings also growing bigger and bigger to the point they can’t be suppressed? Enough for an unspecified number of people to notice.’

No, that person never cared about others’ gazes from the beginning.

‘For me, it’s difficult to do that because I have a lot to lose.’

The moment Jin Seol-woon, who didn’t usually covet others’ things, realized an emotion strange even to himself.

“Gang-hyeon has been good at taking the lead and guiding others since he was young.”

He wanted to extract it from that person’s head and see it. Gang-hyeon-ssi’s past appearance.

What kind of child was he? He must have had a triumphant face then too. He would have been a noisy child who drew attention.

‘It wasn’t just me and Ja Hyeong-woo-ssi looking at him.’

While suffering from the sense of deprivation that he wished Ja Hyeong-woo’s memories were his, he suddenly recalled his own elementary school days. He’d never played with friends after school because he was busy going here and there to vocal academy, dance academy.

Thinking like that, even if he had the past he desired, it seemed like he wouldn’t have had time to hang out and play with little Gyeon Gang-hyeon anyway.

‘…What if I’d suggested we become idols together?’

Jin Seol-woon, who had been having even ridiculous fantasies, soon put away his delusions.

He tried to hope, almost like avoidance, that Ja Hyeong-woo-ssi didn’t like Gang-hyeon-ssi in that way…

But watching from right beside them, there was no way not to know. The only one who didn’t know might be Gang-hyeon-ssi alone.

But lately, hadn’t those two rarely raised their voices in front of others?

Suddenly Ja Hyeong-woo’s phantom glanced back at Jin Seol-woon himself. Their eyes met.

Just like it often happened during training with Gyeon Gang-hyeon between them.

Jin Seol-woon had been certain at some point. That Ja Hyeong-woo was also conscious of him, and that his actions had the intention to keep him in check.

‘If both Ja Hyeong-woo-ssi and I are equal in front of Gang-hyeon-ssi.’

He estimated how many times he’d had his back to Ja Hyeong-woo in battles so far. Himself as a sub-tanker who had once assisted the healer.

Even if it might seem petty, he no longer wanted to help Ja Hyeong-woo from behind. He wanted to stand in front and catch Gyeon Gang-hyeon’s eye.

‘…As Gang-hyeon-ssi said, I should become an offensive tanker, not a support type.’

The announcement, which clearly showed it was written by Gyeon Gang-hyeon himself, revealed his confidence that he would fill the previous leader’s void and successfully clear future stages.

What was unique about this clear attempt was that each successor wasn’t specifically assigned directions. The new leader had boldly removed the four directions entirely from the clear team. He added that, as recent stage difficulty had increased, he declared flexible changes through the leader’s orders regarding that aspect.

As if hinting that writing in directions matching the five elements was merely to provide elements people could relate to.

While entering the stage entrance with Gyeon Gang-hyeon as the leader.

Whispers not caught by cameras leaked between the successors. It was the voices of Sa Ye-ho and Myo Cho-hui.

“Why don’t we get more other successors? General awakeners keep appearing. Where did the tiger and rooster go?”

“Since the former leader left, I feel like one will be filled soon.”

“I hope the tiger comes out. If it’s third among the twelve zodiac, it would theoretically be the strongest dealer. Ja Hyeong-woo is a healer, and Sae-geon hyung isn’t here.”

Chatter that was never allowed when Yoon Sae-geon was leader.

Even so, no anxious feelings seeped into their expressions and voices. Despite currently discussing personnel shortages.

This was the very atmosphere that the one who had called himself a delinquent until now had planted in the current Twelve Zodiac.

Gyeon Gang-hyeon, who had been walking behind listening, finally made a snickering sound.

“The listening monkey and dog are embarrassed. Don’t you know your leader is second from the back in the twelve zodiac?”

Before stepping foot in the entrance, Won Ha-ri looked up at Gyeon Gang-hyeon with a face that still showed her youth. Tension was evident on her face.

‘It’s okay.’

Gyeon Gang-hyeon whispered only with his mouth shape and smiled warmly.

‘I’m confident in you. Today is your stage.’

Soon a mountain thick with trees, where neither bottom nor top could be seen, spread before them.

Since trees covered the black sky, a desolate darkness naturally settled on the ground. It was exactly like the old tale of ‘climbing a mountain at night and meeting a tiger.’

Just as the sound of rustling leaves eerily scratched their eardrums, the growling sound of beasts rang quite close.

But their view was still shrouded in darkness where nothing could be seen.

“Wow shit… It’s just a quantity offensive.”

Sa Ye-ho grumbled lowly. Won Ha-ri grabbed Gyeon Gang-hyeon’s sleeve and trembled slightly.

Before they knew it, between the dense tree trunks, tigers’ yellow eyes were everywhere. The tigers surrounded them as they watched.

Gyeon Gang-hyeon calmly began the strategy.
