Damn Hunter Life Chapter 185

We were just as reckless as the bamboo leaf soldiers.

Yet the fact that no one had died was all thanks to the healers’ hard work.

If Hae Mi-jun and Hyeong-woo’s healing skills were a vast lake, now the two were healing while squeezing out even the dried bottom of that lake.

‘There are still two giants left…!’

We were completely on the defensive. I pondered whether there was another breakthrough.

The bamboo leaf soldiers’ attribute was metal. Metal should properly be subdued by fire—

But I had already used the fire cardinal deity before.

Then the only option was to call upon the same attribute with at least equal power.

“Hyeong-woo, hold onto this!”

I jumped down from the dog.

Gang-hyeon! Hyeong-woo reached out and shouted, but I pretended not to hear.

I did roll once from the failed landing, but got up without major injury.

“Gang-hyeon oppa!”

Won Ha-ri’s face turned white when she saw me. Even so, she didn’t run to me and just kept swinging her sword in place, which made me feel pleased despite the situation.

“Let’s call your cardinal deity! Watch for timing and give me your hand!”

“What? What are you talking about! We don’t match each other!”

Won Ha-ri shouted in confusion, not understanding.

She was right. I’m also ‘yang’, and Won Ha-ri is also ‘yang’. The conditions don’t match.

Unless a new metal attribute successor appeared, the last remaining cardinal deity couldn’t be summoned.

According to the existing rules, that is.

“I’m yang, but I’m also yin.”

Watching Won Ha-ri’s complexion turn strange, I recited playfully.

“I died and came back to life. I’m actually no-attribute.”

“What do you mean died! You’re perfectly alive! Are you going to say such childish things!?”

Won Ha-ri got angry, taking it as a joke. I felt both relieved and bitter.

‘To maintain an awakened person’s body, whether it’s the Twelve Zodiac ‘Dog’ or yang energy, I’m wearing the same things as others.’

Since the original yang energy that should be there is already at rock bottom anyway, then fine, let me try creating some yin energy.

If I broke the system’s rules once, I should definitely be able to do other things too.

I grabbed Won Ha-ri and pulled her into an embrace. The body trapped in my arms struggled. However, perhaps because she was worried I might fall and get hurt, she couldn’t seriously push me away.

“You’re the future of the successors, so your energy can’t be damaged.”

From Won Ha-ri in my arms, I absorbed all the cool, tingling energy.

Because it was yang energy, it was good energy that wouldn’t harm me. However, with a heart similar to taking my own life, I… rejected all the energy that had entered me.

Forcibly twisting something that didn’t fit the rules to make it fit. Intense pain struck as if everything that made up my body was being twisted. I tasted blood in my mouth.

Even so, I succeeded.

To avoid losing consciousness, I embraced Hyeong-woo. I hastily drank the yang energy he gave me.

* * *

At the same time, South Korea was in an uproar over extraordinary news.

[“It has been 6 hours since the successors announced their stage entry.”]

When everyone watching the news had no way of knowing what was happening inside and could only stare at the news with anxious hearts.

A change occurred.

[“Successor Gyeon Gang-hyeon’s world rank has risen. The World Hunter Association has also sent an official document… At this very moment I’m speaking, it has risen again!”]

There had been no previous cases of someone rising several rank levels in such a short period.

[“He has surpassed Successor Ja Hyeong-woo. Korea’s #1 has changed.”]

[“What… what is happening?”]

Even the announcer’s voice trembled with excitement.

The survival of the entire world hung on the narrow time surrounding one person.

But neither ordinary people nor fellow awakened knew this fact.

* * *

Even though the Azure Dragon had destroyed all the soul stones, the body of the giant rolling on the ground was persistent. Parts of the giant kept moving and its arms clattered.

‘There’s still some energy left from the soul stones!’

But since the Azure Dragon had brought all the soul stones to the surface earlier, I just need to find the ones with remaining energy in that pile of dirt and destroy them!

Thinking that, I turned my head toward that pile of dirt—

I gasped. I immediately pushed away Hyeong-woo, who had been pressing his lips to mine.

“Hyeong-woo, I’m fine now so go heal Hae Mi-jun and Won Ha-ri!”

What is that bastard doing!

Jin Seol-woon was digging through the pile of dirt with his sword, finding soul stone fragments.

When he tried to touch them directly, the soul stones repelled him. Even as his bones broke and he became covered in bruises, Jin Seol-woon gritted his teeth and destroyed the soul stones.

“I can destroy them! Why are you doing it!”

The dragon familiar, its body half-collapsed, blocked me from approaching. I stamped my feet behind it and shouted frantically.

“Gang-hyeon-ssi, don’t approach.”

“Why! I’m fine even if I touch them!”

“Hyeong-woo-ssi, ugh, told me. That Gang-hyeon-ssi, gasp, might be in a half-dead state.”

Jin Seol-woon could barely continue speaking, constantly breathing heavily. His completely broken arm stubbornly struck down on the soul stones.

Crack, crunch. Every time he moved his hand, something breaking could be heard. That sound distorted even my heart.

“I heard that, as meaning standing, on the boundary. Like the name of, Gang-hyeon-ssi’s first skill. So Gang-hyeon-ssi… No, if you die…”

The last soul stone fragment scattered with a sharp sound. Jin Seol-woon collapsed softly.

Only then did the dragon let me move forward.

The dragon that had prioritized its master’s body above all now prioritized its master’s commands even when the master became a wreck.

Jin Seol-woon had his eyes closed as if unconscious, but the chat window moved.

Dragon Successor>Me: Why did Gang-hyeon-ssi have to bear such a burden?

==

Even in that moment, he was worrying about me who would remain at the end.

‘Exactly.’

I’m just an extra. I muttered to myself without realizing it.

I sent back Jin Seol-woon and Won Ha-ri too.

Hae Mi-jun was still freezing the giant until then.

Due to excessive skill use, his power went out of control, and he was freezing himself along with it.

Hae Mi-jun’s body was cold as ice. His consciousness was fading, and he couldn’t even hold his head up. Hyeong-woo and I hurriedly helped Hae Mi-jun up and sent him back to school.

After using several intermediate skills, the last giant soldier also fell with a thud to the ground.

“Gasp, huff…”

For a while, only my heavy breathing echoed through the area. The surroundings had become quiet before I knew it.

Finally, not a single living enemy remained in the clearing.

I was alone with Hyeong-woo. Two out of nine people remained.

Hyeong-woo was about to say something. I quickly raised my index finger to my lips and silenced Hyeong-woo.

‘The conquest success message hasn’t appeared.’

With my battered body leaning against Hyeong-woo, I waited. For some change to occur in the empty clearing.

For another ordeal to drive me to the brink.

For an instant, the ground let out a thin cry.

A spine-chilling wailing sound, like the cries of ghosts, spread thinly through the air.

Surrounding me and Hyeong-woo, ghosts sprouted up abundantly.

As if this clearing had been their graveyard from the beginning, and we had summoned them so they all woke from sleep at once.

They all wore pure white burial clothes and had pale faces. Children, women, men, and elderly people were mixed in various ways.

They swarmed toward us. As soon as they moved their legs floating in mid-air, the distance closed instantly.

“They have killing intent!”

I hastily mounted the dog familiar. Hyeong-woo, who had mounted much faster than me, grabbed me and lifted me up in a flash.

The more the ghosts ran through the clearing, the more their numbers increased. It was terrifying how they chased with both arms spread wide.

If we stopped even for a brief moment, we would immediately be torn apart by the ghosts without even a piece of flesh remaining.

The bottle containing the rice wine, the gourd, couldn’t capture that many ghosts. Hyeong-woo grabbed my shoulder and shouted frantically in my ear.

“Bring back the people who went out! It’s better than having no one!”

The teammates I had sent away. They would all have recovered their energy to some extent with healing that gathered the power of regular hunters, and would just be waiting for me to summon them again.

I slowly shook my head.

“No, it’s better to have no one. Because I can’t concentrate when I’m anxious.”

My utterly peaceful eyes met Hyeong-woo’s eyes. Hyeong-woo looked like he’d been hit. He muttered helplessly.

“You… by any chance… you never intended to call them back from the beginning…?”

Soon his pitch-black pupils sharpened menacingly. He gripped my shoulder hard enough to make it ache slightly.

“Summon the others.”

“No.”

“I said summon them!”

“Are you defying the leader you chose?”

I asked back aggressively without avoiding his eyes. While forcefully pushing Hyeong-woo’s chest with my elbow.

Hyeong-woo’s irises shook deeply from within. His fierce momentum died down softly.

Now he pleaded with me desperately.

“I’m sorry. Please don’t send me away too.”

My heart ached, but I didn’t show it outwardly. I forced my will on Hyeong-woo and turned my head away.
