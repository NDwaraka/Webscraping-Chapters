Damn Hunter Life Chapter 161

* * *

Myo Cho-hui was managing the bunker’s air like a ghost, avoiding everyone’s notice. Hae Mi-jun’s eyes had turned bright red as he mechanically continued his tasks. Yang Pyo’s face was also ashen.

Sa Ye-ho, who had gone up to dig through the earth with his dragon familiar and reached near the surface, returned shaking his head.

“I can’t go outside right now either. If I go out, everything will burn up.”

In other words, they were stuck spending more time in the underground bunker.

Every minute and second, while burdening their leader.

Hae Mi-jun hung his head. Tears flowing down his chin dripped onto his collar. Yang Pyo moved his gaze restlessly, fidgeting with his clothes and only sighing.

‘Fuck, this atmosphere could hold a funeral.’

Sa Ye-ho, who had been chewing on jerky, clicked his tongue softly. It was a habit he’d picked up from Yoon Sae-geon. Or maybe not.

He confirmed that Jin Seol-woon had silently emerged from the back and was leaning against the wall. Sa Ye-ho opened his lips lightly.

“If we protect Gyeon Gang-hyeon, don’t you think he’ll protect all of us too?”

Sa Ye-ho looked up above his head. Above was sturdy earth that would never break through, even though the surface had become a burning hell.

Though the owner of that power was currently coughing up blood and half-dying while leaning against Ja Hyeong-woo.

But he truly wouldn’t die. There was no way Gyeon Gang-hyeon would go so easily. They say sickly people live long. Sa Ye-ho convinced himself arbitrarily.

Hae Mi-jun wiped his eyes with his sleeve.

“I don’t really understand what you mean… Are you saying it’s right for Gang-hyeon hyung to sacrifice himself because we protected him?”

“Hey, no, you bastard. I’m saying we trust him.”

Though he was incomparably weaker than Sae-geon hyung, Gyeon Gang-hyeon also had the power to make people believe in him.

“But what’s the point. The important thing is, we’re human trash hiding in a bunker made by the weakest person and just consuming food…”

Sa Ye-ho frowned.

‘What’s with Yang Pyo now. Why did his eloquence suddenly improve. And in such a dark direction.’

Sa Ye-ho grabbed Yang Pyo’s chin and shoved a piece of jerky into his mouth. After making his struggling, mumbling jaw move up and down, he snorted.

“We can’t not eat. Us conserving our strength is helping.”

Then Jin Seol-woon, who had been quiet, reached for dried rations from his inventory. He chewed the food roughly.

Even as attention focused on him, Jin Seol-woon didn’t stop moving his jaw aggressively. He muttered self-deprecatingly.

“I’m not going on broadcasts anymore anyway. At least my expression.”

Silent time passed. During that time, Ja Hyeong-woo didn’t come out from beyond that earthen wall for even a moment. He continuously cared for Gyeon Gang-hyeon without eating or drinking anything.

Gyeon Gang-hyeon continued fighting even while unconscious. He wagered his own body to compete with the boss monster for time.

And he won.

Dawn broke, and the fireballs that had been pounding the ground disappeared like a lie.

[STAGE 33 has been cleared.]

The successors who hadn’t fought directly also clearly confirmed that notification. They were greatly surprised and rushed into the inner room together.

“…!”

At that moment, Gyeon Gang-hyeon, who had been lying there like dead, suddenly sat up.

His vision shook from getting up so suddenly. He could see his teammates with happy faces rushing toward him.

“It’s been cleared!”

“Gang-hyeon hyung…!”

Joyful, somewhat bewildered voices could be heard. All that commotion quickly faded from his ears.

The strength left his wide-open eyes. Gyeon Gang-hyeon collapsed limply.

Before the commotion could start, Ja Hyeong-woo quickly caught him in his arms.

He didn’t remember the situation that followed well. Because Ja Hyeong-woo ignored all procedures and only acknowledged the time from when he laid Gyeon Gang-hyeon on the bed as his own time.

He wiped down the unconscious Gyeon Gang-hyeon’s body with a wet cloth and changed his clothes. In contrast to how he had suffered from the cold inside the stage, his fever shot up instantly on the bed. The state of exhaustion continued for a long time.

His limp fingers didn’t move even once.

Ja Hyeong-woo didn’t look at anything else. His entire world was tied to the powerless hand he was holding. External matters like Association document processing and stage video reviews were all dumped on Sa Ye-ho.

Even if Sa Ye-ho hadn’t blocked the recording content and scenes of Ja Hyeong-woo kissing the sleeping person and doing immoral things were revealed to the world, he wouldn’t have cared.

It was the same as that time. When he had just awakened at the Busan Gate and was spitting blood. Back then too, Gyeon Gang-hyeon had suffered from high fever and couldn’t wake up for three days.

‘It would be good if this isn’t aftereffects, but awakening pains for something new…’

Yet with another part of his heart, he didn’t want to burden Gang-hyeon with more.

If only you had been a completely ordinary normal person.

Everything except Gyeon Gang-hyeon was kicked out of his mind like stones on the road. He stared at only the pale complexion all day.

Even while having such extreme thoughts that he wouldn’t mind killing anything that tried to capture his attention instead of Gyeon Gang-hyeon.

[“I would like you to show me Gang-hyeon-nim.”]

He couldn’t ignore Gang-hyeon’s manager alone.

Ja Hyeong-woo silently connected the status window. He showed the haggard face.

The young man, unable to do anything for himself, was only quiet. He had lost too much weight in just a few days, making his cheeks look somewhat sunken.

‘He worked so hard to gain weight.’

Months of effort had gone to waste. For Ja Hyeong-woo, it was enough that he was alive, but Gyeon Gang-hyeon himself would probably feel dejected when he woke up and touched his gaunt cheeks.

He maintained a calm expression with dead black eyes even while Park Yoon-seok swallowed tears.

[“Ja Hyeong-woo successor-nim. Sa Ye-ho successor-nim.”]

A long silence stretched. Only after Sa Ye-ho, who was beside him, tapped his shoulder did Ja Hyeong-woo realize his name had been called.

[“Would you speak with me for a moment?”]

It was an incomprehensible request. He had nothing to say to Park Yoon-seok. He had only exchanged words occasionally when checking on Gyeon Gang-hyeon’s condition.

If it was about Gyeon Gang-hyeon’s matter, it would be worth listening to, but what was the reason for mentioning Sa Ye-ho as well?

[“You just need to listen. There’s something I’d like you two, who are Gang-hyeon-nim’s friends, to know even now. It’s about the past… but it reaches into the present.”]

Park Yoon-seok began a long story.

It was an unbelievable story. But because it was truth, it carried the sharpness of a blade.

Ja Hyeong-woo’s mind burned completely white.

“He was always in that much pain…”

“Why didn’t this bastard say anything…”

Even Sa Ye-ho’s pale, pressing shouts sounded broken and fragmented.

Ja Hyeong-woo wished someone would tell him what he should do now.

* * *

Hunter School remained silent for a full day. It didn’t convey any news to the outside.

[Silent Hunter School… What’s the truth about the clearing?]

[The situation Korea will face if it failed]

Outside news speculated about everything, either calming or stoking anxiety.

Seeing that regular hunters’ power remained intact, the successors hadn’t all died in battle inside the stage.

This was just normal so far, but even after the successful clearing result came out, no recorded broadcast appeared.

[‘Please reveal the clearing process’ – Netizens trembling with anxiety]

[Was the clearing that difficult… Silent Gyeon Gang-hyeon]

Public opinion aside, the Association’s Kim Jae-moon couldn’t overlook the profits that stage broadcasts brought in. Eventually, he prepared to release the video without even getting Gyeon Gang-hyeon’s permission.

The one who struck Kim Jae-moon in the back of the head was none other than.

[“Even though the person involved, Gang-hyeon-nim, doesn’t want this, I wonder what help it would be…”]

Park Yoon-seok, who had conspired with the successors who couldn’t stand by their leader. He was prepared for the Association’s criticism to pour down on him.

[“I suppose I want to cause trouble at this age too. Following my heart.”]

And Park Yoon-seok’s interview content shook public opinion.

Successor Gyeon Gang-hyeon had fallen into a coma while protecting his comrades in the stage and still hadn’t regained consciousness.

[Many people have really been curious about what kind of person 2nd generation successor leader Gyeon Gang-hyeon was. What kind of person receives such conflicting evaluations, what kind of person is a coward who kicked away awakening with his own feet.]

The deliberately provocative opening led to shocking content.

[Organs deteriorating to the point of coughing blood, symptoms of blood vessels twisting. This is the pain Successor Gyeon Gang-hyeon experienced. No, I don’t mean just the recent few days. A week ago, months ago… and even a year ago.]

He revealed that the current appearance of suffering from excessive combat was identical symptoms to past awakening failure side effects.

[He humbly swallowed the criticism of those who didn’t know. Not wanting to hurt others, he left himself as the villain.]

A person who, despite experiencing that pain, accepted the leader position following others’ expectations.

[Did I say earlier that he experienced this pain from a year ago? No, let me correct that. It was from even earlier.]

Information that only the manager knew was revealed to the world.

It was the story from when young people were first dragged into gates.

A 22-year-old long-term hospital patient disappeared from a certain hospital.

Cheon Gang-hyeon, who had been fighting illness since age fifteen due to an incurable disease inherited from his mother.

While living each day powerlessly between pain and painkillers… he was dragged to a place called Hunter School and met a miracle.

His body, changed by impending awakening, eliminated the disease.

Cheon Gang-hyeon, who became Gyeon Gang-hyeon, dreamed new dreams.

However, inside a certain gate, with a terrified expression, he turned his back on his comrades and missed his awakening.

How brutal the violence that followed was. How much it destroyed one person’s heart.

He also revealed that the harsh words he’d spoken to students back then weren’t drunken rambling but words spoken completely sober. That’s how unwell his mental state was.

[He’s someone who has always fought with life. Someone who got back up after falling and tried to smile.]

Ja Hyeong-woo read every article about Gyeon Gang-hyeon while caring for him beside his bed.

Then when he looked up, there was Gyeon Gang-hyeon with his fever completely gone and breathing comfortably.
