Damn Hunter Life Chapter 127

* * *

Even after the suspicions about the current leader’s tattoo were cleared up, the earth attribute training ground was still bustling with commotion.

Gyeon Gang-hyeon’s voice was the loudest and most boisterous among them.

“Okay fine, let’s say hiding the tattoo was because he was thinking about broadcast mosaics and stuff. But why did he choose a scholar concept?”

“What’s wrong with being a scholar? It suits him fucking well, doesn’t it?”

“It’s too different from the tattoo! Is Woo Sae-geon a pervert? Does he get thrills from living a double life?”

While Gyeon Gang-hyeon was viciously attacking someone who wasn’t even there.

Inside the office of the central building, it was quiet without even the sound of breathing.

Woo Sae-geon, sitting at his desk, was lost in deep thought with his usual expression.

He didn’t bother to explain about the tattoo with his own mouth. This created anxiety among the young successors.

But this incident was also a kind of experiment that Woo Sae-geon had planned.

‘When I, as the person in charge, create suspicion and anxiety among the young successors, who will they gather around and who will they depend on?’

They all flocked to Gyeon Gang-hyeon. Like chicks seeking their mother’s embrace.

Had they forgotten what kind of person Gyeon Gang-hyeon was until this spring? Or had Gyeon Gang-hyeon’s actions up until now erased all of that from their minds?

‘Sa Ye-ho also follows Gyeon Gang-hyeon quite well.’

During the last filming, when Gyeon Gang-hyeon dared to conspire with Sa Ye-ho to lift him up from the sun bed…

Woo Sae-geon didn’t bother to stop it. He showed leniency to Gyeon Gang-hyeon.

Even in that moment, he thought that the time when fear politics was no longer necessary had come.

‘The foundation has all been laid.’

Rather than elevating Ja Hyeong-woo, who was as intimidating as himself, as the next leader to continue the rigid and oppressive atmosphere… perhaps now was the time when ‘affinity’ was more needed.

When his thoughts reached that point, Woo Sae-geon slightly furrowed his brow. A “tch” sound came from his mouth.

‘If only Ja Hyeong-woo hadn’t lost his touch in the first place.’

In the stage, Ja Hyeong-woo couldn’t give him sufficient healing. And that was undoubtedly because he was startled and lost focus seeing Gyeon Gang-hyeon getting crushed by the fireball.

On the other hand—

“Leader! Ja Hyeong-woo’s healing amount is insufficient!”

The guy who always stands one step back with the excuse of being weak, but actually grasps the entire situation and immediately points out when something’s wrong.

Woo Sae-geon rubbed his eye area with his knuckles once and opened the status window.

Even at the end of his reign, work always poured in. The most important among them was the handover to the next person in charge.

==

Would you like to conduct a school vote?

==

The vote of general hunters at Hunter School, which comprises 30% of the next student council president decision vote.

==

Please set the voting candidates.

==

“Those who are 20 years old or older this year.”

Soon the screen showed people who were over 20 this year, one by one in zodiac order. Ja Hyeong-woo, Myo Cho-hui, Sa Ye-ho, Jin Seol-woon—

==

/What about Successor Gyeon Gang-hyeon?

==

The artificial intelligence Status Window, which collects not only school information but all internal and external public opinion, asked a question he hadn’t given.

Woo Sae-geon’s answer was concise.

“Include him.”

The list of voting candidates with five people including Gyeon Gang-hyeon soon spread throughout the entire school.

No matter where you looked around campus, students’ murmuring never ceased.

“How can someone as frivolous as Gyeon Gang-hyeon be a leader?”

“Hey, maybe he’s just included for formality. We’re not going to vote for him anyway.”

“Do votes just roll around like that? Does it make sense to just put him in because it’s boring? The possibility of Gyeon Gang-hyeon actually becoming leader isn’t 0 percent either. That’s what voting is!”

However, even if all the students’ shock were combined, it wouldn’t surpass the magnitude of shock the person in question received.

“Ye, Ye-ho…”

That evening, Sa Ye-ho, who was holding a game console and going beep-beep, faced the zombie-like face of Gyeon Gang-hyeon creeping in through his house window. With a pale face as if he’d seen a ghost somewhere, he suddenly hugged Sa Ye-ho and said:

“I’m seeing things. My name is on the candidate list. They say it’s not an error. Why?”

He’s lost his mind. Sa Ye-ho suppressed his laughter and gently patted Gyeon Gang-hyeon while throwing a bomb into the burning house.

“If there’s a successor vote, I’ll have to vote for Gang-hyeon~”

“Ah fuck, are you crazy?”

The honey-sweet reaction Sa Ye-ho had been hoping for immediately popped out, which was satisfying, but he didn’t know Gang-hyeon would be several times more shocked than that. As if he might collapse right then and there, the blood drained completely from his already white face.

“Can’t you distinguish between when I’m joking and when I’m not? How can such nonsense come out of your mouth? Is there no one else in the world to be leader that they’d make me leader?”

A face burning with anger was thrust in front of Sa Ye-ho’s eyes. Sa Ye-ho, who had been staring blankly, soon burst into a hollow laugh.

A bit annoying, and funny in the midst of it all. It was absurd that Gyeon Gang-hyeon couldn’t grasp reality even at this point.

“Ah, really? You’re serious but I spoke like it was a joke, so sorry. Then shall I speak properly without joking?”

Sa Ye-ho straightened his upper body that had been supporting Gyeon Gang-hyeon’s weight. Then Gyeon Gang-hyeon leaned backward. Sa Ye-ho, who had instantly reversed the situation, clearly pointed out the facts.

“Sae-geon hyung is kinda looking at you as the next leader. Why do you think your name got on the candidate list for no reason?”

Gyeon Gang-hyeon blinked several times. His long eyelashes fluttered anxiously, filled with unease.

“Me, why…”

Sa Ye-ho was swept by the urge to gather Won Ha-ri, Hae Mi-jun, or anyone here as a group and have them beat some sense into this guy with fact violence.

He knew Gyeon Gang-hyeon had been living in some strange delusion about being a delinquent or whatever, but seeing the reality, wasn’t it more serious?

An expression as if he’d lost a country slowly appeared on Gyeon Gang-hyeon’s face. Gyeon Gang-hyeon stammered out excuse-like words.

“I’m, I’m… the Twelve Zodiac villain, right? I used to be a drunk, and now I’m human scum who only says things I shouldn’t?”

Sa Ye-ho let out another hollow laugh.

He seemed smart yet strangely trapped in his own world. Right now that innocent look was reaching its absolute peak.

“Hey, should I ask the kids about that? Ha-ri, Mi-jun, Seol-woon, all of them? Want to see what they’ll answer?”

“Ah, no…”

Gyeon Gang-hyeon stuttered and immediately lowered his tail.

Seeing his lack of confidence in his words, it seemed his objective perspective was slowly opening, but he still wanted to deny it in a corner of his heart as he shook his head and plopped down on the floor.

If you have a long tail, it gets stepped on. Gyeon Gang-hyeon was really sloppy. After helping others here and there, did he think no one would know if he just spoke cleverly? It was more amazing that he truly believed that.

Sa Ye-ho looked down at him warmly and casually threw out a comment.

“You’re similar to Sae-geon hyung.”

Even though he should have heard unspeakable curses immediately, Sa Ye-ho’s thoughts didn’t waver. He had no intention of revising his impression.

So… that’s why his eyes had quickly gone to the changed Gyeon Gang-hyeon.

Since it’s not easy to have big secrets in Hunter School where area CCTV is robust, stories about the leader candidates quickly leaked outside the school as well.

Even if he was just a candidate, the fact remained that a future where Gyeon Gang-hyeon, the Twelve Zodiac’s weak constitution and controversial figure, becoming leader had been discussed.

A tanker in name only who couldn’t tank. A familiar that couldn’t be used well even when present due to internal injuries. A body that couldn’t stand at the forefront as a leader even if he became one. The stamina controversy again.

What good would fusion skills do when the disadvantages were much more prominent?

And past mistakes.

The harsh words he’d spouted to students while drunk, or photos of him crouched and lying on the campus ground resurfaced. Topics about Gyeon Gang-hyeon circulated hotly around the internet.

“…Sigh.”

Park Yoon-seok sighed at the internet boards filled with bitter words.

‘These are things I used to see every day before, so they’re not particularly surprising, but what had been well-forgotten is now.’

Park Yoon-seok, manager of Successor Gyeon Gang-hyeon, resolved that this time he would have to bring out the truth he had buried before.

‘The call records of Gang-hyeon-nim crying and appealing his pain, the remote medical consultation records…’

It wasn’t certain yet. Since no official announcement had been made, this leader position might end up going to Ja Hyeong-woo without incident after all.

However, if a reversal occurred, Park Yoon-seok was prepared to block the fierce criticism that would be poured on his successor with what he had, based on his belief that his successor could do it.

But even while making that resolution, he thought there probably wouldn’t be a need to go against Gyeon Gang-hyeon’s will…

‘The other successors will probably support Gang-hyeon-nim—’

Crash! Suddenly the sound of a cup breaking echoed through the office where Park Yoon-seok was.

“Th-this—!”

Not caring about the glass shards scattered on the floor, a man was trembling and showing the whites of his eyes. What was happening?

“Who… who spread this?”

He was Woo Sae-geon’s assigned manager.

Park Yoon-seok first moved the man who had lost his reason away from the glass shards, then looked down at the monitor screen he’d been looking at.

Real-time hot news. Dozens of article headlines filled the screen.

<Successor Leader Woo Sae-geon, Not Korean? Article Spotted on American ××× News>

<Korea’s #1 Hunter, Woo Sae-geon Revealed to be Korean-American>

<Tattoos and Long Hair? Woo Sae-geon’s Past Photos Become Hot Topic, Background is New York Back Alley>

If this had been the end, perhaps quick damage control would have been possible, but there was worse news after that.
