Damn Hunter Life Chapter 173

An unexpected stroke of luck rolled in.

Among the things I had to do tomorrow morning, the most unpleasant one was resolved. Since it’s Yoon Sae-geon, he’ll definitely keep his word once he’s said it.

I put down my phone and raised my head.

Ja Hyeong-woo, who I thought was sleeping, had his eyes open and was looking at me.

My heart dropped for a moment. His serious gaze without a trace of sleepiness collided with my skin. Ja Hyeong-woo’s mouth opened.

“Sorry. I’m stupid, so I can’t be of any help at times like this.”

Why are you saying that in the middle of sleeping?

“I wish I were a more reliable person.”

And so calmly at that.

Strangely, while receiving Ja Hyeong-woo’s gaze, my heart that had been sharpened with anxiety seemed to become round. The silence that originated from Ja Hyeong-woo was affectionate. The tension that had blocked my throat was washed away.

“Why aren’t you helpful?”

Ja Hyeong-woo looked surprised.

A beat later, I was also startled.

Ah shit. Ah shit… I made a major slip of the tongue. I must have misspoken for a moment because I was struggling and tired.

To recover from this cringiness… I barely managed to cover it up.

“You give me treatment.”

A gentle smile bloomed at Ja Hyeong-woo’s lips. He spread both arms toward me while lying down.

“You’re grateful that I kiss you every day?”

“Won’t you shut up?”

While growling, I hesitantly entered the space between his arms. I was still sleepy and needed a heater.

* * *

The shivering dry body gradually spread into the hot body temperature.

Following the steady breathing, Gyeon Gang-hyeon’s body repeatedly rippled and subsided like calm water.

Ja Hyeong-woo quietly lifted his eyelids to savor the peace given to him.

Gyeon Gang-hyeon was using Ja Hyeong-woo’s arm as a pillow. In his sleep, he placed his outer hand on Ja Hyeong-woo’s stomach.

Ja Hyeong-woo subtly extended his thick ankle. Under the blanket, he slightly entangled it with the white, slender ankle.

Stimulation, excitement, and warmth mixed overwhelmingly, making his body feel hot.

Blood circulated too quickly in his body. The pulsation throughout his body was intense. To the point of having the ridiculous thought that Gyeon Gang-hyeon’s skin might bruise from the pounding beats.

Just looking at someone can make your chest and throat tingle. The sensation of not being able to breathe because someone is precious also exists in this world. There’s also the phenomenon where even the simple action of taking a deep breath becomes addictive when you’re with someone.

He understood that strange truths that could never be realized alone all become reality in this moment.

Gyeon Gang-hyeon’s shallow breathing hit his bare chest. Ja Hyeong-woo gathered his index and middle fingers to stroke Gyeon Gang-hyeon’s soft forehead.

‘He seems comfortable when he’s with me.’

He’s gradually accepting affection. Does he know that his face already softens when looking at me?

When this is over, when I retire, will Gang-hyeon accept me? He makes me harbor expectations.

‘If the weight of the world pressing down on Gang-hyeon decreases.’

Soon Ja Hyeong-woo’s eyes, staring into space, took on a chillingly sharp aura.

He witnessed directly through recent events. That Gang-hyeon had been preparing to handle such situations for a long time.

‘Gang-hyeon was particularly concerned about the future. He tried to train me excessively too.’

Since that was the same even before Gyeon Gang-hyeon became leader, Ja Hyeong-woo vaguely assumed that Gyeon Gang-hyeon might see the future.

‘Or maybe he’s afraid. He doesn’t want to lose the future he finally grasped.’

Even if Gyeon Gang-hyeon never told him anything, Ja Hyeong-woo believed he was a special and excellent person. He believed alone even when no one else believed. That’s why he used to give him sharp criticism in the past.

‘You unconsciously said you’d save me.’

But I’ll save you first.

Ja Hyeong-woo looked at his status window.

* * *

The late-night highway was quiet. The bone-cutting wind of late winter roughly struck the concrete.

On the dry ground beside it, a pitifully crushed form was revealed.

The body already ruined by earth now crumbled even more from passing through the system.

A body that seemed made by clumping earth rose from the ground.

Beom I-ryeong crawled into the nearest visible grass. Even grass contaminated with exhaust and gas was grateful. He sucked in the power contained in the grass.

The surrounding life withered and twisted dry.

Beom I-ryeong, who barely regained the appearance of a boy, slowly opened his eyes.

“I need to push harder.”

* * *

The system that Beom I-ryeong had infiltrated had various functions damaged.

This seems familiar somewhere. Gyeon Gang-hyeon, who was fixing the system like hitting iron with a hammer, eventually recalled.

‘A computer that’s been visited by a hacker? Why is this so realistic?’

Anyway, he organized the messed-up system. He fixed it to work normally again.

‘I think I have more talent for this than expected. Should I learn computers after I retire?’

If I can retire safely. Gyeon Gang-hyeon thought additionally.

Beom I-ryeong was erased from the successor list. Yoon Sae-geon, borrowing Sa Ye-ho’s face, reached an agreement with the Association.

During this time, Gyeon Gang-hyeon managed the school, continued training, and paid attention to the power flowing in his body.

Time until the next stage passed quickly.

The number ahead was 11.

* * *

On a certain day in February, all news in South Korea broadcast breaking news.

[“Gates have occurred simultaneously across the nation. A total of 11 gates have been confirmed, with more than half revealed to be A-rank high-grade gates.”]

[“A total of 26 casualties occurred from gate breaks. All casualties have been transported to hospitals…”]

[“News has come that Successor Gyeon Gang-hyeon will deploy all successors except for two reserves. When successors arrive, regular hunters will cooperate with rescue teams to begin rescue operations.”]

If breaking news talked about damage, other broadcasts had experts come out to discuss the current situation. People trembling with anxiety at home switched channels one by one.

[“With Successor Woo Sae-geon’s retirement, Korea’s 1st place disappeared. All rankings from 2nd place shifted down by one, which seemed like regression.”]

[“However, for nearly 4 months, S-rank hunters’ world rankings have continuously risen. Successors don’t stop growing even at this moment.”]

[“Looking at current successors, it wouldn’t be insufficient to say this is hunters’ golden age.”]

“This way is the evacuation route!”

People rushed toward the direction hunters pointed.

“Ah…”

Black fear filled the eyes of a man who stood still in a daze.

Thick smoke rose from various spots in his vision. Screams for help continued to be heard from half-destroyed buildings.

The gates’ progression was too fast. Fast enough that hideous monsters crawling out of entrances was faster than the system’s matching speed.

Someone frantically called home while running, someone wandered the streets without leaving while looking for friends.

A-rank monsters rushed to devour people, and hunters held out blocking monsters until people could evacuate. Each time they clashed weapons with monsters, scratches spread rapidly across their bodies.

“No!”

Then, another monster burst from the entrance and attacked people who couldn’t escape in time. Hunters jumped in belatedly but were outmatched.

Bang!

Even though time passed, there was no impact on his body. The man quietly opened his eyes.

“Whew—”

There was a tremendous roar as if a truck had collided, but in front of him stood an ordinary person nonchalantly. No, he was too big to call ordinary. And he wore simple-colored hanbok.

The man who had just been rescued couldn’t help but open his mouth as he looked up at Yang Pyo. His head tilted back endlessly as if looking up at Mount Tai.

Following that, a clear voice was gently added.

“Hold it just like that.”

A small frame that seemed like it would only be half of Yang Pyo’s size. However, powerful force was loaded into the spear she held.

Myo Cho-hui descended from above, swinging her glaive in a large trajectory.

She lightly avoided the monster’s claws just by twisting her body, and circled around repeatedly as if sprinting. It was hard to believe she was on the weaker side in mobility among successors, she was so quick.

Unless it’s a spirit monster, life energy flows through monsters too. Myo Cho-hui, the only wood attribute user, could clearly see into the target’s energy. Therefore, she used the opponent’s power in reverse to produce stronger energy than what she possessed.

Even while being led away by the hunter’s hand, the man’s head didn’t turn forward. He couldn’t take his eyes off them.

‘There are only two…?’

For successor fans, meeting successors in person was both luck and tremendous misfortune.

Places where successors would appear were disaster sites where you’d have terrible experiences no matter how well you looked at it.

Nevertheless, if you saw successors like now, not seeing more successors was clearly closer to luck than misfortune.

It meant that even with just two going to one area, it was sufficient to handle gates that regular hunters couldn’t even match.
